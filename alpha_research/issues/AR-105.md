## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-063's macro-surprise/drawdown allocator can be made more orthogonal by separating defensive-stress and relief-rebound sleeves, residualizing/capping each sleeve against retained risk-off/carry/defensive streams, and requiring sleeve-level attribution.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Stress regimes and post-stress recovery regimes are different flows. A defensive sleeve may capture risk reduction/flight-to-quality, while a relief sleeve may capture breadth-confirmed rebound after stress abates. The combined strategy should not simply be another generic risk-off or defensive carry allocator.
- **Expected failure mode:** This becomes a cosmetic extension of rejected AR-072, collapses into AR-037/043/051/063/008/025 correlations, or the relief sleeve repeats rejected AR-073-style relief-rally exhaustion behavior.
- **Falsifier:** Random p25 Sharpe < 0, max correlation > 0.50 to retained risk-off/carry/defensive streams, realized SPY beta above cap, one sleeve dominates performance, or drawdown/turnover fails to improve portfolio utility versus AR-063/AR-072.

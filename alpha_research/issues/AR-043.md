## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Abnormal ETF volume/range/liquidity-stress proxies may identify cross-asset dislocations whose subsequent defensive/risk allocation differs from failed overnight gap reversal.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Stress resolution may be driven by liquidity demand and realized range/volume exhaustion rather than by the sign or size of the overnight gap itself.
- **Expected failure mode:** OHLCV proxies are too noisy for true liquidity, costs erase sparse signals, or the effect is redundant with existing ETF regime/breadth models.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after costs, severe drawdown, or high redundancy versus accepted/watchlist ETF regime alphas.

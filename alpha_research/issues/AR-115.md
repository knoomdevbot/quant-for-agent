## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: During predeclared quarterly reporting-season windows, fixed sector-mapped mega-cap constituents' OHLCV pressure can confirm information diffusion into sector ETFs; sector ETF continuation/reversal may be tradable only when constituent breadth confirms the move.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Constituent abnormal volume, close-location breadth, and residual returns may reveal fundamental-information pressure before it is fully incorporated into sector baskets, while sector ETFs provide lower-turnover tradable exposure.
- Expected failure mode: Coarse earnings-season windows are just generic sector momentum/reversal; result is dominated by one sector/constituent/season; no incremental edge beyond sector ETF-only baseline; too close to prior rejected sector dispersion/earnings variants.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Tradeable universe from SPDR sector ETFs and liquid industry ETFs available through Alpaca/qfa daily bars; signal-input universe from a fixed ex-ante sector-diverse mega-cap constituent map.
- Product-selection filters: Alpaca daily bar availability, liquidity/history, economic sector exposure, fixed constituent-to-sector mapping before performance review.
- Selected universe: To be selected by the research subagent from the candidate pool using coverage/liquidity/economic-exposure filters before performance evaluation.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Tests whether locally promising close-location/abnormal-volume single-name pressure can confirm sector ETF trades; limitations include no actual earnings-calendar/fundamental data and coarse reporting-season proxies.
- Data source: qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only; no CSV; no daemon; no orders; no unavailable earnings calendars or fundamentals.
- Signal features: Predeclared Jan/Apr/Jul/Oct reporting-season windows; constituent abnormal volume; close-location breadth; residual return vs SPY; sector ETF pressure; activation diagnostics.
- Model form: Rule-based cross-sectional sector ETF allocator; constituent symbols are signal inputs only; trade sector/industry ETFs or SHY/no exposure.
- Parameters / search space: Fixed windows and constituent map; 5-10d holding/weekly rebalance; separate continuation and reversal sleeves reported independently; no ex-post blending unless each sleeve clears gates.
- Position sizing: Normalize long/defensive weights to gross exposure <= 1; no leverage.
- Risk constraints: Reject if edge is dominated by one sector, one constituent, one season, or generic SPY/sector momentum/reversal.

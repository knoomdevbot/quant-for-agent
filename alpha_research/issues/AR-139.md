## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Timestamp-safe SEC 10-Q/10-K XBRL companyfacts can identify broad liquid U.S. common stocks with improving operating fundamentals that the market incorporates slowly after the filing date. A post-filing cross-sectional allocator favoring operating cash-flow/accrual, margin, revenue-growth, and share-count inflections may be distinct from OHLCV-only momentum/reversal and ETF calendar families.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Quarterly filings contain standardized public fundamentals. Investors may underreact to multi-metric quality inflections when signals are not reducible to price momentum, low volatility, or earnings-date drift proxies.
- Expected failure mode: SEC companyfacts may be restated or inconsistent across tags; point-in-time reconstruction may be unsafe; signal may reduce to quality/low-vol/value/momentum beta; sparse filing cadence and turnover may dilute returns; concentration risk by mega-cap/sector/year.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Broad U.S. common-stock issuers with unambiguous SEC CIK/ticker mapping and Alpaca/qfa daily OHLCV coverage, initially liquid large/mid-cap names.
- Product-selection filters: common-stock-like assets; exclude ETFs/funds/preferreds/warrants/ADRs where identifiable; minimum price and dollar-volume thresholds; at least 8 recent quarterly observations for key tags; no return-based selection.
- Selected universe: To be selected before scoring. Block/reject if fewer than 150 liquid mapped common stocks or fewer than 500 usable timestamp-safe filing observations survive.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Broad filing-content universe tests a non-price fundamental driver. Limitations: companyfacts endpoint may expose revised history; if facts cannot be reconstructed as-of filed dates, block rather than fabricate.
- Data source: SEC company_tickers/companyfacts endpoints plus Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV. No CSV-backed market data, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: operating cash flow minus net income accrual quality scaled by assets where available, revenue acceleration, gross/operating margin change, inventory/receivables growth vs revenue, diluted share-count reduction / buyback proxy.
- Model form: Interpretable cross-sectional rank allocator with weekly or monthly post-filing refresh.
- Parameters / search space: fixed tag set, filed-date knowledge lag, sector caps/neutral diagnostics, 10 bps cost, limited feature ablations.
- Position sizing: Long top-ranked improving-fundamental names; optional short/underweight deteriorators only if safely modeled; per-name and sector caps; cash allowed.
- Risk constraints: no use of future/restated facts beyond filed date; sector/name/year concentration caps; reject if not timestamp-safe.

## Evaluation Plan
- Random-period protocol: Use broad SEC/qfa overlap with warmup excluded; at least 30 random windows plus leave-one-year-out diagnostics.
- Metrics: full-period and random-window Sharpe distribution, annualized return/vol, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate, activation, filing count, universe breadth, concentration.
- Costs/slippage assumptions: primary 10 bps one-way; 5/20 bps sensitivity.
- Orthogonality checks vs alpha library: compare against AR-010 low-vol quality, AR-005 pairs/stat-arb, AR-003/015/037/049/051 ETF trend/defensive/carry, AR-028/045/056 liquidity reversal, plus price momentum, low-vol/quality proxy, value/size/liquidity buckets, randomized/shifted filing-date placebo, no-filing-calendar baseline, and single-feature variants.
- Acceptance threshold: positive median random-window Sharpe; p25 not materially negative; positive-window rate >=55%; 20 bps does not collapse; beats momentum/quality/sector/size/value/liquidity/placebo baselines; max relevant correlation <=0.60; no single year/sector/issuer dominance.

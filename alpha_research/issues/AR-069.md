## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** A post-gap residual continuation basket may provide an orthogonal mega-cap stream that does not extend the close-location reversal mechanism.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Large overnight/opening repricing with idiosyncratic residual strength can reflect information diffusion rather than liquidity exhaustion. Following residual gaps for 1-5 sessions is behaviorally distinct from fading close-location/abnormal-volume pressure.
- **Expected failure mode:** Daily Alpaca bars approximate gaps poorly without explicit open-to-close execution, the signal is just momentum beta, or turnover/costs dominate.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after 5 bps turnover haircut, correlation > 0.50 to AR-056/AR-045/AR-028, or drawdowns concentrate in high-volatility market regimes.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Large overnight gaps in liquid ETFs may mean-revert during the following regular session or from open to close as opening imbalances are absorbed.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** This tests a different horizon and return driver than AR-002's close-to-close 3-day stretch, focusing on overnight/intraday liquidity imbalance rather than multi-day close momentum exhaustion.
- **Expected failure mode:** Open/close data quality, spread/slippage at the open, changing market microstructure, or signal crowding.
- **Falsifier:** Cost-adjusted open-to-close Sharpe <= 0 across random periods or returns concentrated in a few events.

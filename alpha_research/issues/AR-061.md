## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** A duration/carry allocator driven by ETF-implied curve behavior can produce a return stream less redundant with AR-049's OHLCV-only carry/defensive rotation.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Bond ETF trend, curve proxy spreads, and risk-asset stress regimes may capture duration carry and flight-to-quality premia distinct from equity/commodity defensive ranking.
- **Expected failure mode:** Bond/equity correlations shift in inflation shocks, curve proxy is too crude using ETF prices only, or signal duplicates existing risk-off allocation alphas.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after costs, severe drawdowns in rising-rate regimes, or high correlation to AR-008/AR-037/AR-049.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** US-listed country/region ETFs with stale or low-participation daily action relative to lagged global-risk moves and their own recent range/volume may exhibit short-horizon catch-up or overreaction reversal over the next 1–3 sessions.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Non-synchronous underlying market timing, delayed investor flows, and country ETF liquidity demand can leave some country ETFs under- or over-adjusted versus global risk proxies after the US close. A price-only stale-participation proxy may capture delayed adjustment without requiring unavailable local holiday calendars.
- **Expected failure mode:** The signal may collapse into generic country ETF reversal/momentum, global equity beta, ETF TSMOM, or defensive rotation; turnover/costs may overwhelm any edge; country ETF correlations may make ranks unstable.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Broad country/region ETF pool considered ex ante: EWJ, EWU, EWG, EWQ, EWA, EWC, EWH, EWS, EWT, EEM, FXI, INDA, EWZ, EWW, EZA, EPI, THD, TUR, ILF, VGK, FEZ, MCHI, KWEB, plus any similarly liquid country/region ETFs only if selected by the same coverage/liquidity/history filters before performance review.
- **Product-selection filters:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV availability; sufficient history over the eligible window; adequate dollar-volume/liquidity proxy from OHLCV; country/region economic exposure; minimum selected universe size 10–12 ETFs or mark data-limited/blocked.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by evaluator before performance review using only the filters above.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Country/region ETFs are the natural products for non-synchronous global-market catch-up. Daily ETF OHLCV may not cleanly identify true underlying-market holidays or local-session timing; this issue explicitly avoids unavailable holiday-calendar data and is not a literal holiday strategy.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only. No CSV-backed data, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** Lagged country ETF return/range/volume participation; stale-participation residual versus lagged global-risk controls; optional lagged context controls SPY, EFA, EEM, UUP, GLD used only for signal residualization/diagnostics, not tradable sleeves unless clearly separated.
- **Model form:** Interpretable rule-based cross-sectional ranker with small predeclared parameter grid.
- **Parameters / search space:** Holding period 1/2/3 sessions; stale-participation lookback and z-score windows; range/volume filters; long-only versus constrained long/short variant; turnover cap/no-trade threshold.
- **Position sizing:** Rank-weight selected country ETFs subject to gross exposure normalization and turnover cap.
- **Risk constraints:** Volatility/range filter, max single ETF weight, liquidity filter, turnover cap, no lookahead, no same-day execution leakage.

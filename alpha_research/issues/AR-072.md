## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-063's primary performance can be made more portfolio-useful by explicitly reducing beta, turnover, and redundancy to AR-037/AR-043 ETF regime/stress curves.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** The drawdown-state macro proxy contains some distinct crisis/recovery timing, but unconstrained sleeves overtrade and drift toward existing risk-off/carry allocations.
- **Expected failure mode:** Orthogonality constraints remove most of the return, turnover remains too high, or lower-tail random-window Sharpe stays weak.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after costs, p25 Sharpe remains negative without lower correlation, max drawdown worsens, or max correlation to AR-037/AR-043 remains >0.60.

## Implementation Plan
- **Universe:** Start from AR-063 universe: SPY, QQQ, TLT, IEF, GLD, XLU, XLP, XLV, HYG, LQD, SHY.
- **Data source:** Alpaca/qfa real market data only; no CSV; no `--data-csv`.
- **Signal features:** AR-063 drawdown and shock proxies plus beta estimate, turnover brake, and redundancy penalty against retained ETF stress/carry curves where available.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` returning symbol weights.
- **Parameters / search space:** beta cap 0.3/0.5, turnover brake 5/10 trading days, correlation penalty threshold 0.45/0.55, shock windows 63/126.
- **Position sizing:** gross exposure <= 1.0; capped stress/recovery sleeves; cash-like SHY allowed.
- **Risk constraints:** no live trading; no daemon; document cost/slippage.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** qfa/Alpaca primary backtest plus 6-12 random/stress windows, using the same/overlapping windows as AR-063 where feasible.
- **Metrics:** median/p25/worst Sharpe, annualized return/vol, max drawdown, win rate, turnover proxy, positive-window rate.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5-10 bps one-way external turnover-cost proxy; stress 20 bps if turnover remains high.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare against AR-063, AR-037, AR-043, AR-051, AR-008, and retained watchlist curves.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Better p25 Sharpe or drawdown than AR-063, lower turnover, and max correlation below AR-063's worst comparable correlations.

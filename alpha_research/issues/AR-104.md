## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-015's multi-week ETF time-series momentum edge can be improved or made more portfolio-useful by broadening the product pool and applying ex-ante rolling-correlation cluster risk budgets, rather than adding another simple drawdown/regime brake.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Cross-asset trends persist, but naive ETF TSMOM can overconcentrate in the same risk-on/rates/commodity cluster. A cluster budget may preserve trend alpha while reducing redundant exposure and lower-tail drawdown.
- **Expected failure mode:** The model becomes a redundant AR-015 clone, repeats rejected AR-036 regime-brake behavior, or overconstrains the signal like prior correlation-targeted variants with weaker p25 Sharpe.
- **Falsifier:** Costed median Sharpe materially below AR-015 without lower drawdown/correlation benefit, p25 Sharpe <= 0, max drawdown not improved versus AR-015/AR-036, or max relevant correlation > 0.60 / AR-015 correlation not materially below AR-036's 0.939.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** 20-40 Alpaca/qfa-available liquid ETFs selected ex ante from equity indexes, sector SPDRs, duration/rates, TIPS, credit, commodities, currency proxies if available, and defensive sleeves.
- **Product-selection filters:** Daily history coverage, liquidity, economic sleeve mapping, availability in qfa/Alpaca, and avoidance of near-duplicates inside the same cluster.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed before evaluation from the candidate pool, with explicit sleeve labels and cluster caps.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Broader than the recurring narrow ETF basket; FX/commodity proxies may be limited by Alpaca availability and must be documented.
- **Data source:** qfa with Alpaca real market data only; no CSV-backed research.
- **Signal features:** Multi-week time-series momentum, volatility targeting, rolling realized-correlation clusters, sleeve-level risk budgets, modest drawdown-state gross-exposure taper/re-entry rule.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)`.
- **Parameters / search space:** Momentum lookbacks, volatility windows, cluster correlation window, cluster caps, gross-exposure taper threshold, rebalance cadence, 5/10/20 bps costs.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0; cap cluster exposure; maintain sleeve diversification; no leverage.
- **Risk constraints:** Cluster risk budgeting is risk construction, not a new alpha. Must beat or complement AR-015 on lower-tail/orthogonality, not just look different.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** qfa/Alpaca smoke, primary multi-year window, and 8-12 random/stress windows; compare directly against AR-015 and AR-036 on matched windows where possible.
- **Metrics:** Median/p25/worst Sharpe, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover, positive-window rate, cluster exposure concentration, correlation to AR-015/AR-036.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5/10/20 bps one-way turnover-cost proxies.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** AR-015, AR-036, AR-037/049, AR-051, AR-063, AR-101 once evaluated, and broad-market proxies.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Positive median and p25 costed Sharpe; comparable/lower max drawdown; AR-015 correlation materially below AR-036's 0.939; max relevant correlation <= 0.60 with scrutiny above 0.50.

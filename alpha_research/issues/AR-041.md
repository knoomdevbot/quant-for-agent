## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-021's exchange-session-aware turn-of-month edge may improve if exposure is reduced during high-volatility or risk-off regimes that drove negative random windows.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Month-boundary flow effects can be overwhelmed by broad deleveraging; a simple realized-volatility / trend gate may preserve the flow premium while avoiding stressed regimes.
- **Expected failure mode:** Added gate overfits recent market regimes, reduces sparse positive exposure too much, or remains redundant with AR-021/AR-006.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after documented costs, worse drawdown than AR-021, or no improvement in lower-tail windows.

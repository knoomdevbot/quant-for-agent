## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Country/region ETFs may exhibit persistent relative-strength leadership when global USD stress and commodity/risk context support regional flows; a country-only rotation with USD/commodity context may be less redundant than prior US-centric ETF trend/carry allocators.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Cross-border fund flows, currency stress, and regional commodity/equity risk appetite can move country ETFs in persistent multi-week waves; using UUP/GLD/DBC/SPY only as context may isolate country-flow effects rather than generic ETF momentum.
- Expected failure mode: Signal collapses into global equity beta, generic ETF TSMOM, commodity/inflation rotation, or USD-risk-off exposure; country ETFs may be too correlated and turnover/costs may erase any edge.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Broad liquid international/country ETFs considered ex ante: EFA, EEM, EWJ, EWU, EWG, EWQ, EWL, EWC, EWA, EWH, EWT, EWS, EWY, EWW, INDA, FXI, MCHI, EWZ, EZA, with UUP, GLD, DBC, SPY as context/risk controls only.
- Product-selection filters: Alpaca/qfa daily bar availability, long enough history for 126d lookbacks and random windows, liquidity proxy via volume/dollar-volume, economic exposure to country/region equities. Require at least 10 country/region ETFs; record exclusions.
- Selected universe: To be finalized by evaluator from candidate pool before performance review.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: International/country ETF cross-section was not found in prior local issue searches, but the mechanism overlaps with ETF TSMOM/carry/defensive families; diagnostics must prove incremental country/currency content.
- Data source: qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: Lagged 63d/126d risk-adjusted country ETF returns; drawdown and realized-vol filters; UUP/GLD/DBC/SPY context for USD stress, commodity/risk regime, and global equity beta controls. All features lagged.
- Model form: Rule-based weekly/monthly cross-sectional country ETF rotation with volatility-scaled weights and context/risk gates.
- Parameters / search space: lookbacks 63/126 days; rebalance weekly vs monthly; top-N country ETFs 3-5; volatility window 63d; drawdown brake thresholds; context filter variants with component ablations.
- Position sizing: Long-only or long/cash rotation among selected country/region ETFs; optional risk-off/cash proxy only if predeclared and separately reported.
- Risk constraints: Gross exposure <= 1.0; per-symbol caps; turnover/cost sensitivity at 5 and 10 bps; reject if performance is dominated by a single country or crisis window.

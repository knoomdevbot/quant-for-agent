## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: A narrowly defined set of near-identical, same-benchmark ETF pairs may exhibit small lagged close-ratio residual deviations that mean revert over 1-3 trading days after realistic costs.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Same-exposure ETFs with highly substitutable mandates can temporarily diverge at the close due to microstructure, liquidity, and flow timing. This is tested only as an OHLCV close-ratio residual/stat-arb claim, not as measured NAV premium/discount or fund-flow evidence.
- Expected failure mode: The edge may be too small for 5-10 bps costs, dominated by one pair/group such as SPY/IVV/VOO or GLD/IAU, explained by generic ETF reversal, or redundant with rejected ETF mean-reversion/liquidity-pressure and AR-132 share-class residual failures.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Ex-ante same-exposure ETF groups: SPY/IVV/VOO, QQQ/QQQM, IWM/VTWO, GLD/IAU as primary clean pairs/groups; EFA/IEFA and EEM/IEMG as secondary diagnostics only if benchmark/exposure differences and limitations are documented before performance review. Do not include duration/credit-mismatch pairs such as TLT/EDV or LQD/VCIT in the primary universe.
- Product-selection filters: qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV coverage, sufficient overlapping history, liquidity/dollar-volume suitability, clean same-benchmark or near-identical exposure mapping, and no performance-based pair selection.
- Selected universe: To be fixed by evaluator before performance review from the candidate pool by coverage/liquidity and documented with any exclusions.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Same-exposure ETF pairs are a compact, economically anchored stat-arb test. qfa daily bars do not observe NAV, iNAV, creations/redemptions, fund flows, holdings, or true premium/discount; this is price-return-only fast falsification.
- Data source: qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: Lagged close-to-close log price ratio residuals; rolling 120d/252d z-score or beta/vol normalization using only prior bars; long cheap ETF and short rich ETF within each pair/group.
- Model form: Rule-based pair/group residual mean-reversion allocator with gross-normalized pair spreads and pair/group caps.
- Parameters / search space: zscore_window=[60,120,252], entry_z=[0.5,1.0,1.5], holding_period=[1,2,3], cost_bps_one_way=[5,10] with 2 bps diagnostic only, group_exclusions=[exclude_SPY_group, exclude_GLD_group].
- Position sizing: Dollar-neutral or volatility-normalized pair weights within groups; gross exposure capped; cash when too few pairs/signals are active.
- Risk constraints: Reject if results only work at 2 bps, if a single pair/group dominates, if raw ratio/no-zscore beats the primary, if generic ETF reversal explains the result, or if SPY/IVV/VOO or GLD/IAU alone explains the result.

## Evaluation Plan
- Random-period protocol: Real qfa/Alpaca daily OHLCV evaluation over maximum feasible history with warmup; full sample plus 30-50 random windows where coverage permits.
- Metrics: median random-window Sharpe, p25 Sharpe, worst-window Sharpe, positive-window rate, annualized return, annualized volatility, max drawdown, turnover, activation rate, hit rate, selected pair/group count, group contribution concentration, cost sensitivity, and capacity/liquidity proxy.
- Costs/slippage assumptions: Primary gates at 5 bps and 10 bps one-way; 2 bps may be reported only as optimistic diagnostic and cannot justify watchlist.
- Required ablations: Generic ETF 1-3 day reversal; generic ETF momentum; generic pair-ratio residual baseline; raw ratio/no-zscore baseline; AR-002/011-style ETF mean reversion proxy; AR-121-style OHLCV liquidity-pressure proxy; AR-005-style generic pair residual proxy where comparable; exclude SPY/IVV/VOO; exclude GLD/IAU; primary-only vs secondary-diagnostic pairs.
- Orthogonality checks vs alpha library: Compare/proxy daily returns against AR-002/011 ETF mean-reversion failures, AR-005 pair residual watchlist, AR-121 ETF liquidity-pressure rejection, AR-132 share-class pair residual rejection, AR-015 ETF TSMOM, and SPY/QQQ/GLD/IWM proxies. Mark exact streams unavailable if compact artifacts prevent exact comparison.
- Acceptance threshold: At 10 bps one-way costs, positive median random-window Sharpe, p25 Sharpe not materially negative, positive-window rate >=55%, acceptable drawdown/turnover, no single group dominance, primary beats raw-ratio/generic reversal/generic pair baselines, survives excluding SPY group or is rejected as SPY-specific, and max relevant correlation <=0.60 hard / <=0.50 preferred.

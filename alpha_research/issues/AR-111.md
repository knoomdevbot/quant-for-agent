## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Commodity-linked ETF proxies with lower trailing realized return skewness outperform high-skewness commodity proxies on a monthly cross-sectional basis, creating a qfa-compatible proxy for the commodity futures skewness anomaly.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Investors may overpay for lottery-like positive-skewness commodity exposures, depressing their subsequent returns. Quantpedia cites commodity-futures evidence that a long low-skewness / short high-skewness portfolio earned material excess return not explained by standard commodity benchmarks.
- **Expected failure mode:** ETF proxies are too compromised versus futures, the universe is too small/heterogeneous, returns are driven by one sleeve such as energy/metals, short borrow/turnover costs overwhelm the edge, or the result repeats failed commodity macro/inflation rotations.
- **Falsifier:** 10 bps random-window median Sharpe <= 0, materially negative p25 Sharpe, failure in the pure commodity ETF-only universe, performance concentration in one commodity regime/sleeve, or max relevant correlation > 0.60 to AR-097/AR-099/AR-101 or simple commodity ETF sleeves.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Ex-ante Alpaca/qfa-available commodity-linked ETFs/ETNs selected by liquidity/history and exposure purity, starting from GLD, SLV, USO, UNG, DBA, DBC, CPER, CORN, WEAT, SOYB, PALL, PPLT where data pass. Sector equity proxies such as XLE, XOP, COPX are diagnostic-only expanded-universe ablations, not primary.
- **Product-selection filters:** Daily history coverage, liquidity/dollar-volume proxy, commodity-exposure purity, enough assets for cross-sectional buckets, and no return-ranked universe selection.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed before evaluation; primary universe must prefer commodity-linked ETFs/ETNs over commodity-sector equities.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** This is an ETF proxy test, not a faithful futures replication. ETF/ETN products embed fees, roll costs, collateral/roll methodology, issuer construction, and sometimes equity beta.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real market data only; no CSV-backed research.
- **Signal features:** Lagged 12-month realized skewness of daily returns, monthly rebalance, low-skewness long bucket, high-skewness short/underweight bucket, optional volatility cap/equal-vol sizing as a risk-control ablation.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` cross-sectional monthly allocator.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary 12-month skewness lookback; limited 6/18-month diagnostics; long-low/short-high and long-only/underweight-high variants; 5/10/20 bps one-way turnover costs.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0, equal-weight bucket positions, per-symbol caps, no leverage; negative weights permitted in research but report long-only feasibility separately.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, compact artifacts only; reject if performance depends on sector-equity proxies or optimized lookbacks.

- **Required ablations:** Pure commodity ETF-only universe, expanded universe with sector equity proxies, no energy ETFs, no precious metals, long-only/underweight version, equal-vol/vol-capped version, and 6m/12m/18m lookback comparison.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** AR-097, AR-099, AR-101, commodity shock/inflation proxies, simple DBC/GLD/USO/XLE sleeves, and broad ETF risk-on/risk-off proxies.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Positive 10 bps median random-window Sharpe, p25 preferably >= 0, max relevant correlation <= 0.60 hard / <= 0.50 preferred, survival in pure commodity ETF-only ablation, and no single-sleeve/regime concentration.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: When cap-weight mega-cap leadership becomes unusually stretched versus equal-weight and breadth proxies, subsequent 5–20 trading-day relative mean reversion can favor equal-weight/breadth ETFs over cap-weight leadership sleeves.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: concentration leadership can reflect crowding, index-flow chasing, and temporary breadth deterioration. Once stretch is extreme and volatility is not in crisis, incremental flows may rebalance toward broader exposure or lagging equal-weight proxies.
- Expected failure mode: The signal may be generic size/sector exposure, inverse momentum, or defensive rotation; persistent mega-cap leadership regimes can produce sustained underperformance.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Timestamp-safe monthly Treasury International Capital (TIC) foreign purchases/sales/holdings of U.S. Treasuries and agency securities may predict next-month duration, USD, gold, or credit ETF pressure/reversal after official release lag.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Official Treasury TIC monthly release-date archives, including dated `ticrel_YYYYMMDD.zip` release packages and files such as cross-border portfolio flow history, major foreign holders, and SLT holdings tables; liquid duration/currency/gold/credit ETFs with Alpaca/qfa daily bars.
- Product-selection filters: Use only official dated release archives; require release date/time and as-of month mapping; exclude current revised tables for historical signals; select ETFs by liquidity, data coverage, and economic exposure to foreign Treasury demand, USD, and safe-haven/risk-off pressure.
- Selected universe: Primary plan TLT, IEF, SHY, GOVT, UUP, FXE, FXY, GLD; diagnostics HYG, LQD, SPY if coverage and economic rationale are documented.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Duration ETFs are the most direct tradable exposure; USD and gold ETFs test foreign-demand/risk-off transmission; credit/equity ETFs are diagnostics only. Monthly cadence creates low sample size and regime sensitivity.
- Data source: Official Treasury TIC dated monthly release ZIPs / release-date-specific files for signal; Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV for ETF returns. No CSV-backed qfa market data, no daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: Release-vintage z-scores/changes in foreign net purchases/sales of Treasuries/agencies, major foreign-holder changes, SLT holdings changes by country/sector/security type, and a predeclared foreign Treasury demand-pressure composite.
- Model form: Source-vintage / fast-falsification scout; rule-based monthly allocator if source gate passes.
- Parameters / search space: Lookbacks 12/24/36 monthly releases; one-month holding primary; optional weekly holding diagnostic only if timestamp-safe; 5/10/20 bps cost sensitivity.
- Position sizing: Gross exposure <=1.0; capped duration/USD/gold/risk-off sleeves; cash when no signal; no leverage.
- Risk constraints: Trade no earlier than next tradable session after 4:00 p.m. ET release; no current revised TIC tables; no country/regime cherry-picking; reject if one country, revision month, crisis, or survey benchmark update dominates.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Sharp moves in oil, gold, and broad commodity-linked ETFs can identify inflation/liquidity shocks that lead short-horizon rotation among energy, materials, duration, credit, and broad equity ETFs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** This is deliberately divergent from the rejected crypto-risk-appetite hypothesis: the signal source is commodity/inflation stress rather than BTC/ETH sentiment, and the expected payoff is macro sector/duration rotation rather than crypto-beta spillover.
- **Expected failure mode:** Commodity ETF moves are contemporaneous rather than leading, energy dominates the signal, or the strategy is redundant with existing defensive/carry ETF allocators.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after costs, materially negative p25 Sharpe, poor cost sensitivity, or high redundancy with accepted/watchlist ETF rotation alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Liquid commodity/inflation-sensitive and hedge ETFs: energy, materials, gold, broad commodities where available, duration curve, credit, broad equity, small caps, defensive sectors, and cash-like sleeves.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa real-data availability, liquidity, economic exposure to inflation/commodity shocks, sufficient history, and no ex-post return selection.
- **Selected universe:** Target 20-60 ETFs from XLE, XLB, GLD, SLV, USO/DBA/DBC if available, TLT, IEF, SHY, HYG, LQD, SPY, QQQ, IWM, XLP, XLU, XLV, XLI, XLF.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Uses tradable ETF OHLCV proxies with explicit commodity/inflation exposure; true futures curves or inventory data are out of scope unless a reproducible provider exists.
- **Data source:** Alpaca real OHLCV via qfa only for market-price research; no CSV.
- **Signal features:** 1/5/20-day commodity sleeve returns, volatility-adjusted commodity shock z-scores, oil/gold dispersion, credit confirmation, duration trend filter.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa `generate_signals(context)` allocator.
- **Parameters / search space:** lookback 5/20/60, shock z-threshold 1.0/1.5/2.0, hold 3/5/10 days, max sleeve weight 0.35, rebalance daily/weekly.
- **Position sizing:** Gross <= 1.0, no leverage, SHY/cash-like fallback when signals conflict.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, no secrets, no raw paid data committed.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** qfa/Alpaca smoke test, then 8-12 random/stress windows spanning inflation, disinflation, commodity crash, and risk-on regimes where data allows.
- **Metrics:** Sharpe distribution, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate, positive-window rate, activation rate.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5/10/20 bps one-way turnover cost proxy.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Timestamp-safe public CBOE total/equity/index put-call ratio pressure extremes may forecast short-horizon equity ETF reversal or defensive rotation after a conservative publication lag.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Option-demand imbalances can reflect crowding, hedging stress, and investor sentiment. Extreme equity-vs-index put/call pressure may indicate over-hedging or capitulation that later reverses, or a risk-off state where defensive sleeves outperform.
- Expected failure mode: The signal collapses into VIX/realized volatility, generic ETF reversal, TSMOM, defensive/carry allocation, 2020/2022 regime dominance, stale/non-PIT CBOE files, or VIXY/volatility-product exposure.
- Falsifier: If a reproducible timestamp-safe CBOE historical put/call ratio table with conservative tradability lag cannot be built, block/defer without performance testing. If performance later fails after costs versus VIX/realized-vol controls, ETF reversal/TSMOM, shifted labels, and same-weekday/month placebos, reject.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Broad liquid U.S.-listed ETFs selected by economic exposure to equity risk, sectors, duration, credit, gold, cash-like assets, and volatility hedging: SPY, QQQ, IWM, XLF, XLK, XLY, XLI, XLP, XLV, XLU, XLE, XLB, XLRE, TLT, IEF, HYG, LQD, GLD, SHY, and optionally VIXY only with explicit cost/coverage caveats.
- Product-selection filters: qfa/Alpaca real daily bar coverage; high liquidity; economic exposure; no return-based symbol selection; exclude leveraged/inverse products unless specifically justified and costed.
- Selected universe: To be fixed before scoring after CBOE source feasibility. Initial selected ETF universe should include broad equity, sector, bond, credit, gold/cash sleeves and document coverage limitations.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: The candidate needs ETFs that plausibly respond to option-flow sentiment/risk hedging, but prior ETF regime/reversal results are redundant/hostile; the universe must be fixed ex ante and heavily controlled.
- Data source: CBOE public put/call ratio daily market statistics or official historical files with source URL, selected date, observed/download timestamp where available, and conservative publication-lag policy; qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only if source gate passes. No CSV-backed market data, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: total/equity/index put-call ratio z-scores, equity-minus-index pressure spread, conservative lagged signal availability, optional VIX/SPX ratios as diagnostics only.
- Model form: Feasibility-first external-signal scout; later rule-based ETF allocator with predeclared primary sleeve only after source gates pass.
- Parameters / search space: 63/126/252-day z-score diagnostics; primary predeclared pressure threshold; 1d/5d/10d/20d holds; 10 bps one-way cost plus 5/20 bps sensitivity; no broad ex-post blending.
- Position sizing: Long/defensive rotation or reversal basket with gross exposure cap 1.0; cash when no signal; no leverage.
- Risk constraints: No same-day lookahead; if publication time is unknown use D+1 close or D+2 open availability; reject if sample is too short or dominated by 2020/2022.

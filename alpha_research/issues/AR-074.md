## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Scheduled macro-event liquidity gaps and post-event recovery flows may produce a distinct ETF recovery allocation signal that is not a continuation of AR-064's failed dispersion-compression mechanism or AR-050's rejected convexity/VIXY exposure.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Around FOMC/CPI/payroll-style macro shocks, ETF liquidity can gap and then normalize as hedges are unwound; recovery may be better captured by event/liquidity state than by broad cross-sectional dispersion alone.

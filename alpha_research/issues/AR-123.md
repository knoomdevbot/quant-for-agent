## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Deterministic, publicly known Treasury coupon/principal payment dates create same-day reinvestment demand/support in direct Treasury duration ETFs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Cash paid to Treasury holders may be reinvested into duration exposure on payment dates, creating a short-lived support/drift effect in liquid Treasury ETFs. The effect must be visible on the payment date itself, not rescued by later continuation.
- **Expected failure mode:** The apparent effect is generic duration beta, duration momentum/reversal, turn-of-month/month-end seasonality, Treasury auction/refunding/FOMC/CPI/payroll overlap, one-regime dominance, or sparse-event noise.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** TLT, IEF, SHY, GOVT, TIP, EDV, ZROZ, LQD, HYG, BIL, SGOV.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV coverage, liquidity, sufficient history, economic duration exposure, and pre-performance universe fixation. No return-ranked symbol selection.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent before performance review from direct Treasury duration exposure: likely TLT, IEF, SHY, GOVT, TIP, EDV, and ZROZ if coverage/liquidity pass.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Primary universe should represent Treasury duration/reinvestment exposure. LQD, HYG, BIL, and SGOV are controls/cash/credit diagnostics only unless explicitly justified. Daily OHLCV cannot directly observe reinvestment flows, ETF creations/redemptions, NAV discounts, or intraday support.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV plus deterministic public Treasury coupon/principal payment calendar dates encoded ex ante. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** Fixed Treasury payment-date calendar indicator; optional predeclared diagnostics for month-end/turn-of-month, macro/rates-calendar overlap, duration momentum/reversal, and payment-date clustering. Primary signal must not infer event dates from OHLCV.
- **Model form:** Rule-based event-study allocator active only for payment-date prior close to payment-date close.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary leg is fixed: prior close to payment-date close. Next-day and next-3-day returns are diagnostics only and cannot rescue a failed primary. Selected ETF set must be fixed before performance review.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-weight or simple duration-weighted basket over selected Treasury-duration ETFs, with cash/control diagnostics recorded separately.
- **Risk constraints:** 5 bps primary cost with 10 and 20 bps sensitivity; no leverage; no shorting required.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** 2016-present or max feasible by selected-symbol coverage; event-study windows plus random/year leave-out windows.
- **Metrics:** Median/mean/p25/worst Sharpe or event-return distribution, annualized return/vol where meaningful, max drawdown, turnover, activation/event count, hit rate, positive-window rate, placebo percentile, and 5/10/20 bps cost sensitivity.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5 bps primary; 10 and 20 bps stress.
- **Ablations:** Same day-of-month non-event placebos, random non-event placebos with matched event count/frequency, ex-turn-of-month diagnostic, ex-month-end diagnostic, controls against FOMC/CPI/payroll/Treasury auction/refunding dates where feasible, duration momentum/reversal, unconditional TLT/IEF exposure, and duration beta controls.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare compact/proxy returns against AR-006, AR-021, AR-108, AR-114, AR-120, AR-015, and fixed-income/carry watchlist alphas where compact returns are available; otherwise mark as proxy/deferred due compact artifacts.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Reject on sparse event count, primary 5 bps median event return <= 0, materially negative p25, hit rate below about 55%, placebo percentile below about 0.85, one-year/regime dominance, disappearance ex-ToM/month-end, or high redundancy to existing watchlist alphas. Watchlist requires positive primary after costs and convincing placebo/baseline edge.

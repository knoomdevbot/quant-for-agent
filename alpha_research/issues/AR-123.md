## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Securities with large realized YTD losses can face tax-loss selling pressure into late December and may rebound in early January after that pressure abates.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Tax-motivated selling and window-dressing can create temporary year-end pressure in prior-year losers; pressure may reverse when the tax year closes and new-year risk budgets reset.
- **Expected failure mode:** The effect is just generic January/turn-of-month seasonality, short-lookback mean reversion, beta rebound, or one crisis year; annual event count is too sparse; ETF sleeve has weak tax-loss intuition; universe construction introduces survivorship bias.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Two broad ex-ante sleeves: (1) liquid ETFs across broad index, sector, industry, bond, credit, commodity, international, factor/style, and real-asset exposures; (2) liquid large-cap equities from a static, timestamp-disciplined symbol pool available in the repo or constructed by historical Alpaca coverage/liquidity only, with no current-index survivorship claims.
- **Product-selection filters:** Real Alpaca/qfa daily OHLCV coverage, sufficient history for YTD ranking and January event windows, median dollar-volume/liquidity threshold, no performance-based universe inclusion except the predeclared tax-loss ranking signal, no current constituent backfill.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent before performance review. ETF and large-cap equity sleeves must be reported separately and combined; the primary sleeve rule must be declared before evaluation.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Broad sleeve separation tests whether the tax-loss mechanism is present in individual equities, ETFs, or neither. Static historical coverage/liquidity filters reduce but do not eliminate survivorship and delisting bias. ETF tax-loss intuition is weaker than single-name equity intuition.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** YTD total return/drawdown as of close on the last trading day on or before Dec 20 as the primary signal date; Dec 27-or-prior signal date is diagnostic only. Rank worst YTD performers within each preselected sleeve.
- **Model form:** Rule-based event-calendar reversal basket with diversified loser caps and cash/SPY diagnostic fallback; no hedge optimization.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary trade buys the diversified loser basket at the close of the last trading day of December and holds through the first five January trading days. First 3 and first 10 January trading days are diagnostics only. Basket size/caps and sleeve weights must be predeclared.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-weight or volatility-scaled diversified basket within each sleeve with concentration caps; report ETF sleeve, equity sleeve, and combined sleeve separately.
- **Risk constraints:** 10 bps primary one-way turnover cost; 5 and 20 bps sensitivity; reject if fewer than 8 usable annual events, if one year dominates, or if the result only appears after optimizing entry/exit windows.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** Event-year evaluation across all usable annual late-December/early-January windows; leave-one-year-out robustness; crisis-year exclusion diagnostics. If fewer than 8 usable annual events after filters, reject as data-limited rather than forcing a performance claim.
- **Metrics:** Median/mean/p25/worst event Sharpe or event return distribution where Sharpe is low-power, annualized return/vol if meaningful, max drawdown during event holds, turnover, hit rate, positive-year rate, sleeve-level and combined results.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 10 bps primary; 5 and 20 bps stress.
- **Ablations:** Simple January calendar exposure with no loser ranking; SPY-only January calendar baseline; AR-006/AR-021 turn-of-month and early-month controls; ex-turn-of-month January days; short-lookback mean reversion; ETF TSMOM; low-vol/defensive; equal-weight universe and equal-weight losers; leave-one-year-out and crisis-year exclusion.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare against AR-006/AR-021 turn-of-month seasonality, AR-110 payday/semi-monthly flow, AR-114 municipal tax-calendar/January reinvestment, AR-015/104 TSMOM, defensive/low-vol alphas, and simple loser-reversal/beta proxies where compact streams are available.
- **Acceptance threshold:** At 10 bps, median/event Sharpe or event-return distribution must be positive, p25 not materially negative, positive-year rate above 50%, result must beat January/no-loser, ToM/early-month, mean-reversion, TSMOM, low-vol/defensive, and SPY-only baselines. ETF and equity sleeves must be evaluated separately; do not let one weak sleeve be rescued by the other unless predeclared. Hard fail if max relevant correlation > 0.60 where comparable streams are available.

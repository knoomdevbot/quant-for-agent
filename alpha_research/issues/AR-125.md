## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Large completed-month stock-vs-bond performance gaps create predictable mechanical month-end / turn-of-month rebalancing pressure from balanced funds. A timestamp-safe imbalance estimator should improve over generic turn-of-month exposure by conditioning direction across equity and duration sleeves.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Multi-asset portfolios and balanced funds rebalance toward target weights near month-end. When equities materially outperform bonds, flow pressure should favor bonds and/or create short-lived equity giveback around the month-end window; when bonds outperform, the pressure may reverse. The signal uses only price information known before the trading window.
- **Expected failure mode:** The apparent edge is just generic turn-of-month seasonality, equity/bond momentum, or duration carry; sample size is sparse; costs erase the event edge; returns concentrate in one regime; or the signal is highly correlated with existing ToM/carry/defensive watchlist alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Broad liquid ETFs with long Alpaca daily OHLCV coverage: US equity index ETFs (SPY, QQQ, IWM, DIA), sector SPDRs (XLK, XLF, XLE, XLY, XLP, XLI, XLU, XLV, XLB, XLRE), bond/duration/credit ETFs (TLT, IEF, SHY, LQD, HYG, AGG, BND, TIP if available), and diagnostic controls (GLD, UUP) that must not be selected by performance mining.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca real daily OHLCV availability, multi-year history across 2018-2026 where available, sufficient non-missing bars, liquid ETF exposure matching equity/duration/credit sleeves, and no narrow default basket unless availability forces a documented pilot.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent before performance review from the candidate pool. Minimum intended sleeves: equity indices/sectors plus Treasury/duration/credit ETFs; controls only if coverage and economic rationale are documented.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Selected products should map directly to the rebalancing mechanism: equity sleeve, duration sleeve, credit/risk sleeve, and optional diagnostics. Limitations include no direct flow data, ETF-only proxies, sparse monthly event count, and possible crowding in known month-end effects.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only. No CSV-backed data, no `--data-csv`, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** Prior completed month or prior 20 trading day equity-basket return minus bond-basket return; month-end/turn-of-month trading-window indicators; volatility normalization; optional fixed threshold for imbalance magnitude. All features must be shifted so the trade decision uses only information available before the target window.
- **Model form:** Rule-based event allocator with explicit baselines/ablations.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary predeclared window: last 2 and first 2 trading days around month end. Primary cost: 10 bps with 3/5/10 bps sensitivity. Imbalance lookback: completed calendar month or 20 trading days; threshold fixed ex ante or simple terciles only for diagnostics.
- **Position sizing:** Long/short or long/cash sleeve weights capped by gross exposure; avoid leverage beyond qfa normalized weights; record whether shorts are used for backtest only.
- **Risk constraints:** Gross exposure cap 1.0, no orders/daemon, turnover/cost reporting, reject if the signal collapses into always-long equities/duration or a single regime.

## Evaluation Plan

- **Metrics:** Median Sharpe, p25 Sharpe, worst-window Sharpe, positive-window rate, annualized return, annualized volatility, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate if available, activation/event count, and capacity/liquidity proxy if feasible.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 3/5/10 bps sensitivity; primary decision at 10 bps.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare daily/event return stream to watchlist ToM/carry/defensive alphas, especially AR-006, AR-021, AR-037, AR-049, AR-063 where artifacts are available; report max correlation and limitations. If rejected decisively, orthogonality may be deferred but must be explicitly marked.
- **Required baselines / ablations:** Generic turn-of-month, same-window unconditional equity/bond exposure, prior-month equity-bond TSMOM, prior-month equity-bond reversal, duration/carry proxy, randomized month subsets/placebo dates, and equity-only / Treasury-only / credit-only sleeve attribution.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Positive 10 bps median random-window Sharpe, p25 near or above zero, positive-window rate preferably >=55%, incremental performance over generic ToM and simple TSMOM/reversal baselines, no single-regime dependence, and max relevant correlation <=0.60 (<=0.50 preferred). Reject if gains come only from generic ToM beta.

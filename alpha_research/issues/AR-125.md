## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Liquid dividend-paying U.S. common stocks may exhibit short-horizon rebound or drift after cash-dividend ex/process/payable dates because of dividend-related price adjustment, tax/clientele effects, reinvestment, and liquidity normalization. The primary test must trade only after a conservative post-event decision date and must not rely on unavailable announcement/as-of timestamps.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Ex-dividend mechanics and post-event investor flows may create temporary price pressure or reversal, but much of the apparent effect may be dividend carry, defensive/value sector tilt, or generic short-term reversal.
- Expected failure mode: Signal is explained by dividend yield/carry, sector/market beta, simple momentum/reversal, special dividends, REIT/fund concentration, adjusted-price artifacts, or timestamp safety blocks.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Broad U.S. liquid common stocks with Alpaca cash-dividend corporate-action records and Alpaca real daily OHLCV. Exclude ETFs/funds/preferreds/BDCs/closed-end funds/ambiguous ADRs and isolate REITs/special dividends as diagnostics.
- Product-selection filters: Complete cash-dividend fields, sufficient pre/post daily bars, high pre-event dollar volume, minimum price, common-stock-like asset metadata, event dispersion by symbol/year/sector, and no realized-return selection.
- Selected universe: To be finalized from the broad cash-dividend event pool after data and liquidity filters.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: This is an event-specific common-stock universe, not a repeated ETF basket. Limitations include corporate-action timestamp semantics, dividend-adjusted price accounting, high event clustering in high-dividend sectors, and potential special-dividend contamination.
- Data source: Alpaca real daily OHLCV plus Alpaca cash-dividend corporate-action records. No CSV, no daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: Conservative post-event entry after max(ex_date, process_date) where available; diagnostics for payable/record dates; 1/3/5/10 day post-event drift/reversal legs; pre-event-only liquidity filters.
- Model form: Rule-based post-event corporate-action event study.
- Parameters / search space: Holding windows {1,3,5,10}; costs {5,10,20 bps}; common-stock primary sleeve; REIT/special-dividend/fund diagnostics; optional beta/sector residualization as diagnostics only.
- Position sizing: Equal-weight event sleeves with per-symbol/year concentration caps.
- Risk constraints: No pre-event exposure; no dividend cash claimed unless actually earned during the post-event holding period; reject if performance is concentrated by sector/year/symbol or relies on adjusted-price artifacts.

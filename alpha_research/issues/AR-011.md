## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** The AR-002 3-day ETF mean-reversion effect may only be viable after filtering adverse trend/volatility regimes, volatility-scaling exposures, and explicitly modeling turnover costs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Liquidity-provision reversal can be overwhelmed by persistent trend or stress regimes; conditioning trades may reduce drawdown and cost drag.
- **Expected failure mode:** Filters overfit, reduce sample size, or fail after realistic slippage/fees.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after costs or max drawdown remains materially worse than simple ETF benchmarks.

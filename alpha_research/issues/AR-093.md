## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Extreme crypto downside volatility and BTC/ETH correlation spikes can signal imminent de-risking/quality flight into duration, gold, utilities/staples/healthcare, or cash-like ETFs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Crypto stress events often reflect leverage unwind and liquidity demand; spillovers may appear in credit/equity beta before slower allocators rebalance.
- **Expected failure mode:** Signal only fires during rare episodes, crypto-specific crashes do not transmit, or defensive assets fail when rates/inflation dominate.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after costs, p25 Sharpe materially negative, unstable across random windows, unavailable external data with timestamp discipline, or high redundancy with existing ETF momentum/defensive alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Crypto stress transmission and quality-flight products: crypto proxies where supported, high-beta tech/semis/fintech/crypto-linked equities, broad equity ETFs, credit ETFs, duration curve ETFs, gold/commodities, defensive sectors, low-volatility/quality proxies, and cash-like sleeves.
- **Product-selection filters:** Historical data availability, stress sensitivity, defensive/quality exposure, liquidity, and ex-ante mapping to crypto deleveraging/quality-flight mechanism.
- **Selected universe target:** 30-100 products after filtering; include risk assets likely to be reduced and defensive assets likely to receive flows.
- **Seed universe examples:** SPY, QQQ, IWM, TLT, IEF, SHY, GLD, HYG, LQD, XLP, XLU, XLV, plus liquid high-beta/crypto-linked equities or ETFs if data-supported.
- **External signal source:** Real crypto OHLCV provider plus Alpaca/qfa real ETF OHLCV; no CSV-backed market-price research. Must use strictly lagged crypto data available before ETF rebalance timestamp.
- **Signal features:** BTC/ETH drawdown z-score, realized-vol spike, BTC/ETH correlation/dispersion, credit stress confirmation using HYG/LQD, defensive sleeve momentum filter.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` returning symbol weights; external signal ingestion must be deterministic, cached only as compact derived features if allowed, and documented.
- **Parameters / search space:** stress z-threshold 1.5/2.0/2.5, lookback 20/60 days, hold 3/5/10 days, defensive sleeve cap 0.60, turnover brake.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0, no leverage, max single ETF/stock weight per config, SHY/cash-like fallback allowed when signal confidence is low.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, no raw paid/vendor data committed, no secrets, no lookahead from revised external data. If external data cannot be obtained reproducibly, mark blocked rather than fabricating a proxy.

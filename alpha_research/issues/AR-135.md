## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Weekly CFTC Commitments of Traders positioning extremes and 4-week changes, used only after public release, can identify crowded futures exposures that unwind over the next 5-20 trading days in related U.S.-listed ETFs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Futures positioning can reflect slow institutional crowding and risk-budget pressure. When non-commercial positioning is extreme and price momentum diverges or stalls, crowded trades may unwind across ETF proxies before the full positioning stress is absorbed.
- **Expected failure mode:** The signal may be stale, noisy, dominated by generic commodity/rates/USD/equity momentum or reversal, too concentrated in one sleeve/regime, or unavailable with clean timestamp/release-lag discipline.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Broad ex-ante ETF pool mapped to CFTC futures markets: commodities/metals/energy/agriculture (`GLD`, `SLV`, `USO`, `UNG`, `DBA`, `DBC`, `PDBC`, `GSG`), rates/inflation (`TLT`, `IEF`, `SHY`, `TIP`), dollar/currency ETFs if data/liquidity allow (`UUP`, `FXE`, `FXY`), and equity index proxies (`SPY`, `QQQ`, `IWM`, `DIA`).
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa daily bar availability, liquidity/volume proxy, economic mapping to a CFTC reportable futures market, sufficient history overlapping COT releases, and no performance-based symbol selection.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent after data availability and mapping checks, with separate primary mapped-sleeve universe and diagnostics for expanded/removed sleeves.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** The universe must be selected for futures-market exposure mapping, not for prior alpha performance. Currency/commodity ETF coverage may be limited; commodity ETFs may include roll/issuer effects that weaken futures-positioning mapping.
- **Data source:** CFTC public COT legacy/disaggregated weekly reports with explicit as-of rule using only information after public Friday release; ETF prices/returns from Alpaca/qfa real daily market data only.
- **Signal features:** COT non-commercial/net positioning z-scores, 4-week positioning changes, positioning-extreme flags, price-momentum divergence/stall filters, sleeve-normalized ranks.
- **Model form:** Rule-based weekly/daily allocator with explicit parameters.
- **Parameters / search space:** COT z-score lookback 52-156 weeks, extreme threshold 1.0-2.0, positioning change lookback 2-6 weeks, ETF price momentum divergence lookback 20-63 trading days, holding period/rebalance 5/10/20 trading days or weekly.
- **Position sizing:** Dollar-neutral or capped long/short sleeve allocation where feasible; gross exposure capped; individual symbol cap recorded.
- **Risk constraints:** Cap gross and per-sleeve exposure; avoid symbol selection based on returns; record borrow/short limitations and run long-only diagnostic if shorts are not feasible.

- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** Primary 10 bps per turnover side unless the evaluator justifies a different conservative value; include 5/20 bps sensitivity if feasible.

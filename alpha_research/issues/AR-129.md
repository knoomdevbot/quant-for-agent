## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** The AR-127 pre-holiday risk-on leg has a small but measurable ETF calendar premium that may survive after simplifying to broad equity/credit risk-on sleeves and enforcing stronger robustness/overlap gates.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Pre-holiday risk appetite and reduced liquidity can create short-lived equity/credit risk-on drift before public exchange holidays. The effect should be tested as a narrow event premium, not a broad holiday search.
- **Expected failure mode:** The AR-127 result is sparse-sample noise, family/year concentration, an early-close/daily-bar artifact, generic ToM/OPEX/payday/month-end exposure, or too small after 20 bps costs.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Broad risk-on ETFs only: liquid US equity index/style, international equity, and credit ETFs. Sector/commodity/energy sleeves are diagnostics only unless predeclared as attribution.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa daily OHLCV coverage, high liquidity/dollar-volume proxy, long coverage from 2018 where possible, direct risk-on/credit exposure, no return-based inclusion.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent before performance review. Preferred simple primary universe: SPY, QQQ, IWM, MDY, EFA, EEM plus HYG/LQD/VCIT or an even simpler equity-only and equity+credit split.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Simplification reduces AR-127 sleeve-selection risk and tests whether the edge exists in broad risk-on exposures rather than sector/commodity selection. ETF universe is current-symbol and may have survivorship/availability limitations.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV plus public NYSE holiday/early-close calendar rules. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** Last tradable session before predeclared NYSE full-day holidays as primary. Early-close-adjacent events are reported separately; post-holiday legs are excluded from the tradable hypothesis.
- **Model form:** Rule-based event-calendar long risk-on basket; no optimizer; cash/zero exposure outside event windows.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary window fixed to the last tradable pre-holiday session. No holiday dropping or window optimization. 10 bps primary cost; 5/20 bps sensitivity.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-weight or equal-risk capped basket; report equity-only, credit-only, and equity+credit diagnostics.
- **Risk constraints:** Reject if 10 bps median/hit rate fails, if ex-calendar-overlap subset fails, if placebo rank is weak, if one year/holiday family is necessary, or if early-close separation reveals an untradeable artifact.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** Event-window evaluation across usable years; leave-one-year-out, leave-one-holiday-family-out, early-close-adjacent vs non-early-close separation, matched month+weekday placebo dates, random event subsets if feasible.
- **Metrics:** Mean/median/p25/worst event return, event Sharpe, hit rate, annualized return/vol if meaningful, max drawdown, turnover, activation, positive-year/family rate, placebo rank.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 10 bps one-way primary, with 5 and 20 bps sensitivity.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Controls/ablations vs AR-006/AR-021 ToM, AR-106 OPEX/rebalance, AR-110 payday, AR-125 month-end, AR-015 ETF TSMOM, defensive/carry proxies, and same-window unconditional risk-on exposure.
- **Acceptance threshold:** 10 bps full-sample median event return > 0, hit rate >=55%, ex-calendar-overlap subset median >0 with p25 near zero or better, placebo rank >=0.85 preferably >=0.90, no single family/year dependence, and max relevant correlation <=0.60 hard.

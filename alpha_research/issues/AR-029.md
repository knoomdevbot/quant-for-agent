## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** The low-volatility quality proxy may become more robust if turnover, rebalance cadence, and parameter stability are explicitly optimized instead of relying on a single positive but modest pre-cost configuration.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** AR-010 showed modest positive random-window Sharpe but costs were only documented. A direct robustness pass should test whether the edge survives practical turnover haircuts and neighboring parameters.
- **Expected failure mode:** Costs, crowding, regime shift, data limitations, parameter fragility, or redundancy with stronger watchlist alphas.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after documented/applied costs, strongly negative lower-tail windows, or high redundancy without improvement.

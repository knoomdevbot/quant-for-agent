# AR-137 — Non-levered commodity ETF term-structure/roll-proxy allocator

## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Within non-levered liquid commodity ETP families, lagged daily-OHLCV relative performance of front-month-like products versus longer-dated, broader, or same-commodity proxies can serve as a noisy but timestamp-safe proxy for roll-yield / term-structure pressure. A weekly or monthly allocator that requires both favorable carry-proxy rank and modest price-trend confirmation may outperform commodity beta and simple commodity ETF TSMOM after costs.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: Commodity ETP construction embeds futures-roll, collateral, fee, and curve-shape effects. Persistent contango/backwardation and roll-pressure differences can appear in relative ETF returns before they are fully captured by broad commodity momentum. Combining carry-proxy and trend may avoid mechanically long negative-carry sleeves.
- Expected failure mode: The proxy may be too noisy or too close to commodity momentum / energy beta; surviving universe may be too sparse; broad commodity ETFs may differ by methodology rather than roll yield; turnover/costs may overwhelm signal amplitude; performance may be dominated by one commodity sleeve or regime.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Broad ex-ante U.S.-listed non-levered commodity ETFs/ETNs with Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV availability: oil/fuel candidates USO, USL, BNO; natural gas UNG and UNL if available; precious/industrial metals GLD, IAU, SLV, CPER, DBB; agriculture DBA, CORN, WEAT, SOYB; broad commodity DBC, PDBC, COMT, GSG. Explicitly exclude leveraged/inverse products such as UCO, SCO, BOIL, KOLD from the primary universe.
- Product-selection filters: pre-test filters only: Alpaca/qfa data availability, sufficient overlap since the selected start date, liquidity/volume sanity, non-levered/non-inverse structure, clear commodity exposure, and minimum viable breadth.
- Selected universe: To be selected by the evaluator from the candidate pool before performance scoring. Hard block/reject if fewer than 10 non-levered commodity products survive or fewer than 3 sleeves survive across oil/gas, metals, agriculture, and broad commodity.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Candidate pool is idea-specific and commodity-only, designed to test a roll/carry proxy rather than broad ETF seasonality. Limitations: daily OHLCV cannot observe true futures curves or roll schedules; ETF distributions/fees/methodology differences can contaminate the proxy.
- Data source: Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV only. No CSV, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: lagged relative returns within predeclared commodity families / proxy pairs, carry-proxy ranks, 60d and 120d trend confirmation, realized volatility for sizing/caps, sleeve caps, and liquidity/coverage masks.
- Model form: interpretable rule-based cross-sectional allocator with weekly or monthly rebalance.
- Parameters / search space: fixed primary settings with limited diagnostics: carry lookback 60/120d, trend lookback 60/120d, rebalance weekly/monthly, 10 bps primary cost, volatility target/cap, per-symbol and per-sleeve caps. No performance-optimized family remapping.
- Position sizing: Long/underweight commodity sleeves where carry proxy and trend agree; cap gross exposure and per-sleeve concentration; allow cash when signals are weak.
- Risk constraints: no leveraged/inverse products in primary universe; no shorting if evaluator cannot model borrow/short constraints safely; per-symbol and per-sleeve caps; reject if one symbol/sleeve/year dominates.

## Evaluation Plan
- Random-period protocol: Use broad eligible period from available overlapping real daily OHLCV; run at least 30 random or walk-forward windows with enough warmup, scoring after costs.
- Metrics: median Sharpe, mean Sharpe, p25 Sharpe, worst-window Sharpe, positive-window rate, annualized return, annualized volatility, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate if available, activation rate, sleeve/year concentration, and capacity/liquidity proxy where available.
- Costs/slippage assumptions: primary 10 bps one-way turnover cost; include sensitivity if feasible.
- Orthogonality checks vs alpha library: correlate daily/window return stream against watchlist/accepted alphas where compact artifacts permit; specifically compare against commodity ETF TSMOM, AR-097/AR-099/AR-101 inflation/commodity shock variants, AR-111 commodity skewness, AR-135 COT positioning, broad commodity beta, and DBC/GLD/USO beta controls.
- Acceptance threshold: positive median after-cost Sharpe; p25 Sharpe positive or not materially negative; positive-window rate materially above chance (target >=55%); combined signal beats carry-only, trend-only, equal-weight commodity basket, DBC/GLD/USO beta controls, and commodity ETF TSMOM on robustness; max relevant correlation below 0.60; no single sleeve/year dominance; compact artifacts and no leakage/CSV/order violations.

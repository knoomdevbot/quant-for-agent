## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** After broad market drawdowns stop worsening, large liquid single names with positive sector/market residual momentum, resilient relative volume/breadth, and controlled realized beta/volatility may continue outperforming over 5-20 trading days.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Post-stress stabilization can reveal idiosyncratic leadership as forced de-risking abates. The intended driver is sector/market residual leadership and breadth resilience, not generic low-vol quality, earnings drift, or ETF macro relief.
- **Expected failure mode:** The signal degenerates into rejected low-vol quality AR-029/AR-030 or rejected macro drawdown/residual relief AR-072/AR-105; performance concentrates in mega-cap tech; costs and turnover erase residual momentum.
- **Falsifier:** 10 bps random-window median Sharpe <= 0, p25 Sharpe < 0 without exceptional orthogonality, max correlation > 0.50 to AR-029/AR-063/AR-072/AR-105, attribution dominated by low-vol/beta rather than residual leadership, or 20 bps Sharpe non-positive due turnover.

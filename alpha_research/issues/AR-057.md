## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Mega-cap equities that break out after multi-day range compression and rising volume may exhibit short-horizon continuation, a different return driver from AR-045's liquidity-reversal fade.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Volatility compression can reflect temporary uncertainty or dealer hedging balance; a decisive range/volume expansion may trigger trend-following and risk-budget reallocation.
- **Expected failure mode:** Breakouts are false in mean-reverting mega-caps, costs/turnover overwhelm edge, or the signal becomes a market beta clone.
- **Falsifier:** Non-positive median random-period Sharpe after 5 bps turnover haircut, high market/AR-045 correlation without better robustness, or large drawdowns in reversal regimes.

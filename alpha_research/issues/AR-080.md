## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Cross-asset realized-correlation breakdowns can identify allocation regimes distinct from AR-072's rejected drawdown/beta/redundancy-constrained macro allocator.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** AR-072 retained too much correlation to existing stress/carry alphas; correlation-regime breakdowns may capture when normal stock/bond/credit/gold relationships stop diversifying and require a different allocation response.
- **Expected failure mode:** Correlation breaks are lagging/noisy, risk-off allocation converges back to existing defensive/carry alphas, or low-frequency rebalances miss regime turns.

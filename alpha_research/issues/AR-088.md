## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Upside/downside realized-volatility asymmetry across equity, duration, credit, and gold ETFs can identify defensive allocation regimes without relying on AR-080's failed realized-correlation breakdown mechanism.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Persistent downside-vol dominance can signal deleveraging pressure and convex demand for defense, while upside-vol dominance may mark re-risking; this is a volatility-shape signal rather than a cross-asset correlation signal.
- **Expected failure mode:** Realized-vol asymmetry is lagging, redundant with drawdown/momentum alphas, or allocations become too defensive and underperform in rebounds.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Daily FINRA Reg SHO short-sale volume files may identify temporary one-sided short-sale pressure in liquid common stocks. When a stock has unusually high short-volume share relative to its own history and contemporaneous negative residual price pressure, the next 1-5 trading days may rebound as liquidity demand normalizes.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: FINRA daily short-sale volume is public, timestampable, broad, and directly tied to trading pressure. It offers a market-microstructure flow proxy distinct from OHLCV-only ETF calendar/momentum/residual/commodity/weather/COT ideas.
- Expected failure mode: FINRA short-volume is not true short interest and includes market-maker/order-routing artifacts; signal may collapse into generic reversal/liquidity reversal; release timing may require conservative next-session lag; edge may concentrate in distressed/illiquid/meme regimes; costs may overwhelm short horizon.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Broad liquid U.S. common stocks with Alpaca/qfa daily bars and FINRA CNMS daily short-sale-volume coverage.
- Product-selection filters: common stocks only; exclude ETFs/funds/ADRs/preferreds/warrants/OTC/leveraged/inverse where identifiable; fixed minimum price, dollar-volume, coverage, and mapping thresholds before scoring.
- Selected universe: To be selected before scoring. Block/reject if fewer than 150 liquid common stocks or fewer than 5000 timestamp-safe signal events survive.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Broad microstructure source avoids narrow default ETF baskets. Limitations: ticker mapping and release-time semantics must be conservative.
- Data source: FINRA Reg SHO daily short-sale volume files plus Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV. No CSV-backed market data, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: lagged ShortVolume/TotalVolume, own-history short-volume-share z-score over 60/120d, prior-day residual return vs SPY/sector proxy, abnormal dollar volume and volatility controls, liquidity/price/event-count masks.
- Model form: Long-only short-horizon event/reversal allocator.
- Parameters / search space: next-session timestamp lag, high short-volume z-score threshold, negative residual pressure threshold, 1-5d holds with fixed primary, 10 bps primary cost.
- Position sizing: equal-risk baskets of liquid stocks; cash when too few names qualify; no primary short leg unless borrow/short constraints can be modeled safely.
- Risk constraints: next-session lag after FINRA file date unless release time is verified; symbol/sector/year concentration caps; meme-regime exclusion diagnostic.

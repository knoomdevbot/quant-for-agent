## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-049's positive but modest cost-adjusted performance can be improved by reducing daily turnover and strengthening the orthogonality brake without relying on a single parameter point.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Carry/defensive ETF allocation may retain regime value, but AR-049's 11.96x annualized turnover proxy leaves too much edge exposed to execution friction.
- **Expected failure mode:** Lower turnover removes the signal edge, or correlations to AR-037/AR-015 remain too high.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after 5-10 bps costs, worse drawdown than AR-049, or max correlation to retained watchlist alphas above ~0.60 without better performance.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-046's basket-level liquidity exhaustion recovery can be made more robust by trading only in regimes where broad liquidity shocks historically mean-revert rather than trend, with explicit sparsity and turnover constraints.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Forced-selling recoveries are regime dependent; a market-trend/volatility gate may avoid crisis continuation while preserving recovery baskets after temporary liquidity demand imbalances.
- **Expected failure mode:** Regime filters overfit, reduce sample size too much, or fail to reduce AR-028/market correlation.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after 5 bps turnover haircut, positive windows remain <=50%, or drawdown/turnover worsens versus AR-046 without lower redundancy.

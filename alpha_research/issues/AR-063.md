## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** A drawdown-state and macro-surprise proxy allocator can produce a return stream distinct from AR-051's momentum/correlation defensive rotation.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Markets often over/under-react around macro stress and recovery states; an allocation based on realized drawdown state and ETF proxy shocks may capture crisis/recovery premia.
- **Expected failure mode:** Macro surprise proxies are unavailable or too noisy with OHLCV-only data, signal duplicates existing risk-off/stress alphas, or whipsaw costs dominate.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after costs, severe drawdown, or high redundancy with AR-008/AR-043/AR-051.

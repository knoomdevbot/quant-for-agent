## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-051's robust random-window Sharpe can be retained while lowering redundancy by setting correlation targets on held-out return streams rather than using only an in-model proxy brake.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Defensive ETF rotation works in multiple regimes, but its portfolio value improves only if it adds non-duplicative exposure versus TSMOM/carry watchlist alphas.
- **Expected failure mode:** Explicit orthogonality constraints reduce returns, or true correlations remain around 0.6+.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after 10 bps costs, p25 Sharpe deteriorates materially, max drawdown worsens, or max correlation to retained watchlist curves remains high without compensation.

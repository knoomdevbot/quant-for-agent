## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Large moves in liquid prediction-market probabilities for macro/political outcomes can identify event-risk repricing that spills into equity, duration, gold, and sector ETFs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Prediction markets aggregate event expectations continuously; probability shocks may capture information flow before or alongside traditional markets, especially around Fed policy, elections, recession/inflation, tariffs, and geopolitical risk.
- **Expected failure mode:** Polymarket markets are sparse, regime-specific, too retail/noisy, or timestamps/market-selection create lookahead/data-snooping risk.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after costs, p25 Sharpe materially negative, unstable across random windows, unavailable external data with timestamp discipline, or high redundancy with existing ETF momentum/defensive alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Universe:** SPY, QQQ, IWM, TLT, IEF, SHY, GLD, HYG, LQD, XLE, XLU, XLV.
- **External signal source:** Polymarket public read-only APIs for market probabilities/history plus Alpaca/qfa real OHLCV; no CSV-backed market-price research. Market selection must be specified ex ante and recorded.
- **Signal features:** Daily probability changes, probability volatility, event-cluster breadth, surprise z-scores, risk-on/risk-off mapping to ETFs, liquidity/volume filter for prediction markets.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` returning symbol weights; external signal ingestion must be deterministic, cached only as compact derived features if allowed, and documented.
- **Parameters / search space:** probability-change z-threshold 1.0/1.5/2.0, lookback 10/30/60 market days, market-volume floor, hold 1/3/5/10 days, max sleeve weight 0.40.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0, no leverage, max single ETF/stock weight per config, SHY/cash-like fallback allowed when signal confidence is low.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, no raw paid/vendor data committed, no secrets, no lookahead from revised external data. If external data cannot be obtained reproducibly, mark blocked rather than fabricating a proxy.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** External-signal availability check, qfa/Alpaca smoke test, then 6-12 random/stress windows using strictly point-in-time or lagged external features.
- **Metrics:** Median/p25/worst Sharpe after costs, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover proxy, win rate, positive-window rate, activation rate.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** Minimum 5-10 bps one-way turnover-cost proxy; higher stress if signal changes frequently or uses less liquid ETFs.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare against AR-003/015 ETF TSMOM, AR-028/045 closing-volume reversal, AR-037/049 defensive carry, AR-051/063 stress/carry variants, and recent cross-asset ETF alphas where compact returns exist.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Positive median and acceptable p25 random-window Sharpe after costs, low redundancy, reproducible external-signal pipeline, and no obvious timestamp leakage.

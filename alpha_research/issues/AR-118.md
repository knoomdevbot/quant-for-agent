## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** A short-horizon pullback-recovery effect exists inside defensive/carry ETFs that remain in a positive intermediate trend, especially during predeclared benign/moderate stress. The timing overlay should add incremental value beyond pure carry/defensive ranks and ETF TSMOM/defensive-rotation baselines.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Within non-crisis but somewhat pressured regimes, liquid defensive/carry ETFs can experience temporary flow-driven pullbacks while their intermediate trend remains intact. Buying the strongest recovery setups may capture liquidity normalization without simply holding the usual carry/defensive basket.
- **Expected failure mode:** The pullback score is only a noisy/high-turnover variant of existing AR-037/049 carry/defensive allocation; the 63d trend filter recreates AR-015/051 exposures; the benign/moderate stress gate overfits; costs erase the edge at 10-20 bps; or max correlation exceeds 0.60 to retained ETF watchlist alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Pre-performance Alpaca/qfa coverage/liquidity screen over 25-40 ETFs: SHY, IEF, TLT, GOVT, TIP, LQD, HYG, GLD, SLV, DBC, USO, DBA, SPY, QQQ, IWM, DIA, USMV, QUAL, SPLV, XLU, XLP, XLV, XLE, XLF, XLI, XLB, XLRE, XLC, plus only similarly liquid/available ETFs if needed.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca daily history from 2018-present where available, bar coverage, liquidity/volume sanity, and exposure diversity before performance review. Document dropped symbols and selected universe.
- **Selected universe:** To be selected by the research subagent from the candidate pool before performance review.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Broad enough to test defensive carry, duration, credit, commodity, broad/style equity, and sector sleeves; limitations include ETF inception history, Alpaca data coverage, and potential redundancy with existing ETF regime/carry alphas.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only; no CSV; no `--data-csv`; no daemon; no orders.
- **Signal features:** Positive 63d risk-adjusted trend / return-per-vol eligibility; 5d pullback z-score plus rebound/stabilization setup; ex-ante benign/moderate stress gate, e.g. SPY 20d return roughly -8% to +2%, realized volatility below crisis threshold, HYG/LQD not in severe breakdown.
- **Model form:** Rule-based ETF allocator, long-only top 2-4 ETFs or cash/SHY fallback.
- **Parameters / search space:** 63d trend lookback; 5d pullback/rebound lookback; weekly rebalance; 5-10d hold; stress gate thresholds; top N 2-4; turnover brake/cooldown.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-weight or score-weighted top 2-4 long-only ETF sleeve with cash/no-exposure fallback.
- **Risk constraints:** Turnover brake, crisis-state exclusion, max sleeve concentration, cash fallback, cost sensitivity at 5/10/20 bps.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** Primary 2018-present where coverage supports it plus random windows from 2018-present including benign, moderate-stress, inflation/rates, and recovery regimes.
- **Metrics:** Median/mean/p25/worst Sharpe, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover, activation rate, positive-window rate, sleeve attribution.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5, 10, and 20 bps one-way turnover-cost proxies; 10 bps primary decision threshold.
- **Ablations:** No stress gate; no pullback/rebound score (pure 63d trend); no trend filter (pullback only); simple carry/defensive rank; cash/SHY fallback variant.
- **Baselines / orthogonality checks:** AR-015, AR-037, AR-049, AR-051, AR-060, AR-061, AR-067, AR-071, AR-105, simple carry rank, simple ETF TSMOM rank. Reject automatically if max relevant corr >0.60, preferred >0.50.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Median random-window Sharpe >0 after 10 bps and preferably after 20 bps; p25 >=0 or only mildly negative with compelling low correlation/drawdown; positive-window rate materially above 50%; turnover viable after costs; full model must outperform no-gate/no-pullback/no-trend and simple carry/TSMOM baselines.

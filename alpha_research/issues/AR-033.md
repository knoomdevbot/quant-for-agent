## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Waiting for opening-range failure before fading ETF gaps may avoid trend-day continuation and reduce open-auction slippage versus immediately fading all gaps.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** AR-012 failed open-to-close after costs; confirmation may filter the worst gap-continuation days.
- **Expected failure mode:** Without intraday bars the evaluator is only approximate, or median cost-adjusted random-window Sharpe remains <= 0.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Credit ETF spread-proxy compression/expansion breadth can identify risk-transfer regimes that allocate across credit, equities, duration, gold, and defensive sectors.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** This is deliberately divergent from the rejected crypto stress-to-quality hypothesis: the return driver is credit-market breadth and spread-proxy state, not crypto deleveraging. Credit risk appetite may be closer to institutional allocation flows and less noisy than BTC/ETH stress.
- **Expected failure mode:** HYG/LQD/Treasury ETF ratios are stale or redundant with equity momentum, duration shocks dominate credit signals, or costs erase the edge.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after costs, p25 Sharpe materially negative, unstable across rate regimes, or high redundancy with existing defensive/carry alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Liquid credit, duration, equity, sector, gold, and cash-like ETFs: HYG, JNK, LQD, VCIT, TLT, IEF, SHY, SPY, QQQ, IWM, XLF, XLI, XLE, XLU, XLP, XLV, GLD, plus other liquid fixed-income ETFs if data-supported.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa availability, liquidity, spread-proxy economic exposure, history across credit stress/recovery periods, and diversity across risk/defensive sleeves.
- **Selected universe:** Target 20-50 ETFs selected ex ante from the candidate pool by data availability and exposure mapping.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** ETF price ratios are proxies for credit spreads and may confound rates/duration; true OAS data is out of scope unless a reproducible source is available.
- **Data source:** Alpaca real OHLCV via qfa only; no CSV-backed research.
- **Signal features:** HYG/LQD and HYG/IEF relative returns, credit breadth across available credit ETFs, volatility-adjusted compression/expansion z-scores, equity confirmation, duration hedge state.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa `generate_signals(context)` allocator.
- **Parameters / search space:** lookback 10/20/60, breadth threshold 0.55/0.65/0.75, z-threshold 1.0/1.5/2.0, hold 5/10/20 days, max sleeve weight 0.35.
- **Position sizing:** Gross <= 1.0, no leverage, SHY/cash-like fallback when credit breadth is neutral.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, no secrets, no raw paid data committed.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** qfa/Alpaca smoke test, then 8-12 random/stress windows spanning credit stress, recovery, rising-rate, and equity bull regimes.
- **Metrics:** Sharpe distribution, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate, positive-window rate, activation rate.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5-10 bps one-way turnover cost proxy.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare to ETF defensive rotation/carry, TSMOM, and stress-liquidity alphas where compact return streams exist.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Positive median and acceptable p25 Sharpe after costs, controlled drawdown/turnover, and low redundancy.

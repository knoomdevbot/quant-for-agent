## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Perpetual swap funding can be a real, crypto-native carry source, but only if funding cashflows are modeled directly and crowded OI acceleration is filtered before it becomes liquidation/trend risk.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Leveraged perp traders pay/receive funding to balance perp and spot. Extreme funding may compensate liquidity providers/carry traders, while sharp OI acceleration plus price trend can indicate unstable crowding where carry is overwhelmed by directional/liquidation risk.
- **Expected failure mode:** Funding carry is just a momentum/crash-risk proxy; OI filter overfits; net edge disappears after maker/taker fees, slippage, funding timing, venue outages, and delisting/missing-data treatment.
- **Falsifier:** If timestamp-safe funding/OI/price data with adequate breadth cannot be obtained, mark blocked. If performance testing later fails to beat plain funding carry and crypto TSMOM/reversal after maker/taker fees and slippage, reject.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** BTC, ETH, SOL and top liquid USD/USDT perpetuals across major venues such as Binance, OKX, Bybit, Coinbase International, and Kraken where historical funding/OI/mark/index/last-price data is available.
- **Product-selection filters:** minimum historical funding and OI coverage, stable contract metadata, minimum volume/open-interest/depth, no severe missing intervals, venue reliability, no performance-based selection.
- **Selected universe:** feasibility scout should first report coverage; performance universe is selected only after data gates pass.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** broad crypto derivatives pool matched to the funding/OI mechanism; qfa's current stock/ETF Alpaca path is insufficient unless extended or paired with a crypto real-data evaluator.
- **Data source:** real exchange or vendor data only: exchange APIs, CoinGlass, Kaiko, Amberdata, Coin Metrics, or equivalent. No CSV fixtures or fabricated OHLCV carry proxies.
- **Signal features:** funding rate level/rank/z-score, funding accrual timing, OI growth/deceleration, price momentum/reversal controls, liquidity/depth filters.
- **Model form:** feasibility-first scout; later rule-based cross-sectional/TS carry model if data gates pass.
- **Parameters / search space:** funding lookbacks, OI acceleration/deceleration windows, rebalance interval, maker/taker fill mix, slippage stress.
- **Position sizing:** capped gross/net exposure, per-symbol caps, venue caps, liquidity-scaled weights.
- **Risk constraints:** max turnover, max single-asset exposure, skip missing/unstable contracts, conservative data availability lag.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** scout first verifies data; later test random windows across bull/bear/crash/sideways regimes.
- **Metrics:** Sharpe, Sortino if available, annualized return, vol, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate, funding PnL vs mark/price PnL, positive-window rate, capacity/depth proxy.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** mandatory maker/taker model: `maker_bps`, `taker_bps`, `fill_mix`; include 1x/2x/3x fee and slippage stresses; funding cashflows included at correct timestamps.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** compare to accepted ETF TSMOM/defensive/liquidity alphas if returns are commensurable; otherwise mark deferred during scout.
- **Controls / ablations:** plain funding carry, OI filter only, price TSMOM, price reversal, shifted funding/OI timestamps, venue-excluded diagnostics, BTC/ETH-only diagnostics.
- **Acceptance threshold:** feasibility passes only if data coverage and timestamp discipline are adequate; later alpha pass requires positive costed median random-window Sharpe, p25 not materially negative, positive-window rate >=55%, and superiority to plain funding carry/TSMOM.

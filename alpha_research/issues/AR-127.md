## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Public NYSE holiday and early-close calendars create predictable short-horizon risk appetite / liquidity patterns in broad ETFs: risk-on drift before holidays and possible normalization after holidays.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Dealer/investor positioning, reduced liquidity, and pre-holiday risk appetite can create a short-lived calendar premium distinct from turn-of-month, OPEX/rebalance, payday, or month-end effects.
- **Expected failure mode:** The effect is generic turn-of-month/OPEX/payday/month-end exposure, one-holiday/year concentration, intraday early-close behavior not visible in daily bars, or daily OHLCV ETF proxies are too noisy after 10 bps costs.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Broad liquid ETFs across equity beta, sectors, credit, duration, gold/commodities, energy, cash/defensive, and broad index sleeves.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa real daily OHLCV coverage, sufficient history from at least 2018 where possible, high liquidity/dollar-volume proxy, direct economic exposure to risk-on/risk-off, duration, credit, commodity, and defensive sleeves; no return-based inclusion.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent before performance review, with separated sleeve reporting for equity beta, sectors, credit, duration, gold/commodities, energy, and cash/defensive.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Public exchange-calendar dates are timestamp-safe and ex ante. Daily bars cannot isolate intraday early-close effects, and holiday samples are sparse; sleeve results must not be post-hoc optimized.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV plus public NYSE holiday/early-close calendar dates embedded as ex-ante event dates. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** Predeclared event flags for the last tradable session before regular full-day holidays and first tradable session after holidays. Early-close sessions may be diagnostic only unless predeclared as a separate sleeve.
- **Model form:** Rule-based event-calendar allocator with fixed sleeve directions; primary risk-on pre-holiday exposure and separately reported post-holiday normalization/reversal diagnostics.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary: pre-holiday last-session window; post-holiday first-session diagnostic. No optimized holiday dropping. 10 bps one-way primary cost; 5/20 bps sensitivity if feasible.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-risk or equal-weight sleeve baskets with caps; report single-sleeve and combined diagnostics.
- **Risk constraints:** Reject if fewer than a viable count of event windows, if one holiday/year dominates, if placebo rank is weak, if costs erase the effect, or if ToM/OPEX/payday/month-end controls explain returns.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** Event-window evaluation across all usable years; leave-one-year-out, leave-one-holiday-family-out, matched placebo dates by weekday/month/frequency, and random event subsets.
- **Metrics:** Median/p25/worst event-window Sharpe or event-return distribution, annualized return/vol where meaningful, max drawdown during event holds, turnover, hit rate, positive-event/year rate, activation, sleeve-level attribution.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 10 bps one-way primary, with 5 and 20 bps sensitivity if runtime permits.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Hard controls and proxy correlations vs AR-006/AR-021 turn-of-month, AR-110 payday/semi-monthly flow, AR-125 month-end imbalance, AR-106 OPEX/rebalance, AR-015 ETF TSMOM, and defensive/carry baselines.
- **Acceptance threshold:** Positive 10 bps median event/random-window Sharpe, p25 not materially negative, positive-window rate above ~55%, placebo rank strong, no single holiday/year concentration, and incremental performance after ex-ToM/ex-OPEX/ex-payday/ex-month-end controls.

Defensive diagnostic sleeve: TLT, IEF, SHY, GLD, UUP, XLU, XLP.

- **Ex-ToM/OPEX/payday/month-end subset:** 34 events; mean +0.2417%, median +0.1242%, p25 -0.0342%, hit rate 61.76%, event Sharpe sqrt252 7.27.
- **Placebo/control checks:** matched month+weekday placebo rank 0.901; cost sensitivity median +0.1396% at 5 bps, +0.0896% at 10 bps, -0.0104% at 20 bps. Control medians: ToM +0.0236%, OPEX -0.0246%, payday -0.0450%, month-end +0.0235%. Proxy correlations to combined daily stream were low (about 0.02-0.13).
- **Post-holiday diagnostic:** defensive leg failed: 78 events, mean -0.1542%, median -0.0912%, hit rate 38.46%, event Sharpe sqrt252 -6.66. Combined pre-risk-on + post-defensive stream is not recommended (annualized return -0.72%, Sharpe -0.28, max drawdown -9.55%).
- **Limitations:** daily bars cannot isolate intraday early-close behavior; holiday sample is sparse and includes observed full-day exchange-closure gaps; current-ETF universe may have survivorship.
- **Suggested decision:** `advance_to_refinement` for the primary pre-holiday risk-on sleeve only; drop or quarantine the post-holiday defensive leg as a negative diagnostic.

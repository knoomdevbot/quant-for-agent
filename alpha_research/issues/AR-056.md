## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** AR-045's cost-aware close-location/abnormal-volume reversal can be improved by explicitly reducing market/AR-028 overlap, using stricter edge thresholds and beta/market-regime gates while preserving the lower-turnover benefit.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Some of AR-045's returns may be broad mean-reversion beta. Orthogonalizing against market stress and requiring stronger idiosyncratic close-location events may retain liquidity-reversal edge with lower redundancy.
- **Expected failure mode:** Orthogonality constraints remove too many profitable trades, leaving sparse/noisy signals; the edge remains a clone of AR-028.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after 5 bps turnover haircut, AR-028 correlation remains >0.45 without superior turnover/drawdown, or positive returns concentrate in one window.

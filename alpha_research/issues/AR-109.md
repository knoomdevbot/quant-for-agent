## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Abrupt multi-day relative dislocations between ETF-implied real-rate proxies, nominal duration, gold/USD, and defensive/risk sleeves can overreact to real-rate repricing. After lagged stabilization, a 5-15 day allocator may capture mean reversion without relying on generic duration, gold, dollar, or risk-off beta.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Real-rate repricing can force rapid cross-asset repositioning across duration, inflation hedges, USD, credit, and equity-risk sleeves. A lagged, relative shock-fade design may isolate temporary overreaction better than generic carry/defensive rotation.
- **Expected failure mode:** The signal is just long-duration, long-gold, short-dollar, or risk-off beta; it duplicates AR-037/AR-049/AR-063/AR-105; lower-tail random windows are negative; or cost sensitivity erases the apparent edge.
- **Falsifier:** Reject if median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after 10 bps, p25 materially negative, max correlation > 0.60 to AR-037/AR-049/AR-063/AR-105 or simple duration/gold/USD/equity proxies, or attribution shows generic beta rather than a real-rate-relative shock fade.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Alpaca/qfa daily OHLCV ETFs selected ex ante: nominal duration `TLT`, `IEF`, `SHY`; inflation-linked / real-rate proxy `TIP`; gold/USD `GLD`, `UUP`; credit/equity risk and defensive proxies `LQD`, `HYG`, `SPY`, `XLU`. Optional additions only if data availability/liquidity and economic exposure are documented before evaluation.
- **Product-selection filters:** Clean Alpaca daily coverage, liquidity/dollar-volume proxy, economic exposure to real-rate/duration/inflation/USD/risk sleeves, and no return-ranked product selection.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed before evaluation from the candidate pool; expected primary set is `TLT`, `IEF`, `SHY`, `TIP`, `GLD`, `UUP`, `LQD`, `HYG`, `SPY`, `XLU` if coverage is clean.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** OHLCV ETF proxies are indirect; they cannot observe real-rate futures, TIPS breakevens, or cross-asset positioning directly. The idea must be framed as a price-derived proxy test, not a macro-data claim.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real market data only; no CSV-backed research.
- **Signal features:** Lagged TIP-vs-TLT relative shock, TLT/IEF/SHY curve-duration shock, GLD-vs-TIP divergence, UUP confirmation, SPY/XLU/HYG/LQD defensive/risk confirmation, 1-3 bar stabilization via realized range/close-location/reversal confirmation, and 5/10/15 day holding variants.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` cross-asset allocator.
- **Parameters / search space:** Shock lookbacks 3/5/10 days, stabilization windows 1/2/3 days, hold periods 5/10/15 days, volatility/risk-off filters, sleeve caps, gross exposure cap, and 5/10/20 bps costs.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0, no leverage, capped sleeves; avoid concentrated single-proxy bets.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, compact artifacts only; hard reject if generic duration/gold/USD/equity beta explains returns.

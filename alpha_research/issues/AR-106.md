## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Liquid ETFs experience temporary price pressure around option-expiry and major rebalance windows, and a strictly event-gated daily OHLCV confirmation signal can capture short-horizon reversal without becoming another generic turn-of-month or broad close-volume reversal model.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Option expiry, quarterly expiry, and index/rebalance weeks can concentrate dealer/institutional flow. When abnormal volume, extreme close-location, and failed follow-through occur inside these predeclared windows, short-lived pressure may mean-revert over 1-3 sessions.
- **Expected failure mode:** The effect is only AR-006/AR-021 turn-of-month seasonality in disguise, repeats rejected broad liquidity-reversal failures such as AR-103/AR-095, turnover overwhelms the short-horizon edge, or activation is too sparse outside a few crisis windows.
- **Falsifier:** 10 bps random/stress median Sharpe <= 0, materially negative p25 Sharpe, returns disappear after excluding standard turn-of-month windows, turnover too high for event windows, or max relevant correlation > 0.60 to AR-006/AR-021/AR-103/liquidity-reversal proxies.

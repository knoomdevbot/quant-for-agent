## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Liquid ETFs experience temporary price pressure around option-expiry and major rebalance windows, and a strictly event-gated daily OHLCV confirmation signal can capture short-horizon reversal without becoming another generic turn-of-month or broad close-volume reversal model.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Option expiry, quarterly expiry, and index/rebalance weeks can concentrate dealer/institutional flow. When abnormal volume, extreme close-location, and failed follow-through occur inside these predeclared windows, short-lived pressure may mean-revert over 1-3 sessions.
- **Expected failure mode:** The effect is only AR-006/AR-021 turn-of-month seasonality in disguise, repeats rejected broad liquidity-reversal failures such as AR-103/AR-095, turnover overwhelms the short-horizon edge, or activation is too sparse outside a few crisis windows.
- **Falsifier:** 10 bps random/stress median Sharpe <= 0, materially negative p25 Sharpe, returns disappear after excluding standard turn-of-month windows, turnover too high for event windows, or max relevant correlation > 0.60 to AR-006/AR-021/AR-103/liquidity-reversal proxies.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Ex-ante liquid ETFs across broad/index, sector, style/factor, bond/rates/credit, and commodity/real-asset sleeves, for example SPY, QQQ, IWM, DIA, VTI, XLK, XLF, XLE, XLV, XLI, XLY, XLP, XLU, XLB, XLRE, IWF, IWD, MTUM, VLUE, QUAL, USMV, TLT, IEF, SHY, HYG, LQD, GLD, SLV, USO, DBA when history/liquidity pass.
- **Product-selection filters:** Daily history coverage, liquidity/dollar-volume proxy, economic sleeve diversity, event-window bar availability, and no return-ranked universe selection.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed before evaluation from the candidate pool; event windows and exclusions declared ex ante.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Uses exchange-calendar and option-expiry calendar rules plus daily OHLCV proxies; no direct option-flow, creation/redemption, or auction imbalance data.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real market data only; no CSV-backed research.
- **Signal features:** Third-Friday OPEX week and quarterly expiry/rebalance windows, explicit ex-turn-of-month control, lagged abnormal volume z-score, close-location percentile, 1/3-day event pressure return, failed continuation/reversal confirmation, realized-vol state as sizing/skip filter only.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` with event-gated activation only.
- **Parameters / search space:** Event window offsets, 1/3/5-day holds, volume z-score thresholds, close-location thresholds, reversal/failed-continuation thresholds, volatility skip/sizing bands, 5/10/20 bps costs.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0, sleeve caps, per-symbol caps, no leverage; hold only inside/after predeclared event windows.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, compact artifacts only; reject if performance depends on standard ToM windows.

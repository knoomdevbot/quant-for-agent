## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** The decay speed of volume-normalized range shocks over 2-10 sessions can identify whether ETF stress is resolving, producing a different allocation edge than AR-043's same-day stress breadth allocation.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Markets may reward exposure when liquidity shocks heal quickly and penalize exposure when shocks persist; half-life of stress decay is a different return driver from instantaneous stress level.
- **Expected failure mode:** Half-life estimates are noisy in daily OHLCV, too lagged, or redundant with simple trend/volatility signals.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-period Sharpe <= 0 after costs, unstable half-life parameter behavior, or high correlation with AR-043/AR-008.

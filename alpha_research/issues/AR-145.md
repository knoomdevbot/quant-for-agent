## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Conservative one-session-lagged public CBOE volatility-index term-structure and vol-of-vol signals may identify risk-appetite and volatility-risk-premium regimes not fully captured by ETF OHLCV.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: VIX term-structure slope, short-dated inversion, and VVIX stress can reflect hedging pressure and volatility-risk-premium dislocations. Steep contango / low VVIX may favor risk-on equity and credit sleeves; backwardation, VIX9D/VIX inversion, high VVIX, or post-inversion normalization may favor defensive or rebound allocations.
- Expected failure mode: The signal may collapse into simple VIX level, ETF trend/reversal, crisis beta, or already-rejected defensive/carry/stress allocators; VVIX/VIX9D histories may be short or missing; one crisis window may dominate.

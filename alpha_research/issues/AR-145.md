## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Native spot-crypto majors may exhibit daily/weekly trend persistence and short-horizon reversal regimes that are not captured by prior crypto-to-ETF spillover tests, if evaluated directly on real crypto bars with explicit maker/taker costs.

- Expected failure mode: Price-only crypto momentum/reversal is generic, crowded, regime-dependent, dominated by buy-and-hold beta, or destroyed by maker/taker costs and 24/7 turnover.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Alpaca/qfa-supported liquid spot crypto majors, starting with BTC/USD and ETH/USD; SOL/USD may be included only after real-data coverage/liquidity gates pass.
- Product-selection filters: real Alpaca crypto bars, continuous sufficient history, sane volume coverage, supported slash-symbol format, liquidity/coverage before performance, no return-based selection.
- Selected universe: BTC/USD and ETH/USD primary; optional SOL/USD only if coverage gate passes.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Direct native crypto test is materially different from AR-092/AR-093 crypto ETF-spillover rejections and provides a required benchmark/control for AR-144 exchange-flow ideas. Small universe limits cross-sectional breadth; treat as fast-falsification baseline, not broad portfolio evidence.
- Data source: qfa/Alpaca real crypto OHLCV only. No CSV-backed data, no qfa daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: predeclared daily 20/60-day time-series momentum blend, weekly rebalance, volatility scaling/risk brake, optional explicitly defined 3-5 day overshoot dampener as a predeclared reversal control.
- Model form: rule-based crypto spot allocator; long/flat or long/short only if evaluator and instrument support are explicit.
- Parameters / search space: primary 20/60-day trend blend; diagnostics for 10/20/60/120 day lookbacks only as robustness, not post-hoc selection; weekly rebalance; UTC daily-bar signal lag to next completed bar.
- Position sizing: volatility-scaled BTC/ETH weights, capped gross exposure, cash on insufficient history/missing bars.
- Risk constraints: maker/taker fees, crypto 365-day calendar reporting or dual 252/365 conventions, asset concentration diagnostics, no daemon/orders.

## Evaluation Plan
- Random-period protocol: Random and regime windows across crypto bull, bear, high-vol, low-vol, and chop periods after coverage gate; report both full-sample and random-window distributions.
- Metrics: median/p25/worst random-window Sharpe, positive-window rate, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover, hit rate, activation rate, asset contribution.
- Costs/slippage assumptions: primary mixed fill maker 2 bps / taker 5 bps or conservative spot fee schedule supported by qfa; stress 0 bps, 1x, 2x, 3x, and taker-only. Include funding only if perps are used; default spot-only has no funding.
- Orthogonality checks vs alpha library: Compare to AR-092/AR-093 crypto ETF-spillover failures, ETF TSMOM AR-003/AR-015, BTC/ETH buy-hold/equal-weight, simple 20d/60d momentum, 3-5d reversal, and volatility-targeted buy-hold.
- Acceptance threshold: Positive post-cost median random-window Sharpe; p25 preferred >= -0.10 and not materially negative; positive-window rate >=55%; beats BTC/ETH equal-weight and simple momentum/reversal baselines after fees; not dominated by one crypto bull regime or one asset; no timestamp/leakage defect.

## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Timestamp-safe ETF distribution/ex-dividend events create short-horizon pressure, drift, or recovery effects in liquid income, bond, REIT, and defensive ETFs that are not explained by generic carry, duration, month-end, or ETF TSMOM exposures.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** ETF distribution events can trigger mechanical price adjustments, dividend-capture positioning, reinvestment flows, and temporary liquidity pressure. If event dates and cash amounts are known before the trading decision, a rule-based event allocator may capture pressure/recovery while avoiding generic carry clones.
- **Expected failure mode:** Corporate-action data are unavailable or not timestamp-safe; dividend cash/price adjustment handling creates false P&L; any signal is just carry/duration/month-end exposure; turnover/costs erase the effect; performance concentrates in one ETF family, rates regime, or calendar month.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Pre-performance pool of liquid income/defensive ETFs with expected distribution activity and Alpaca daily bar coverage: HYG, LQD, SHY, IEF, TLT, GOVT, TIP, EMB, MUB, VCIT, VCSH, BND, AGG, XLU, XLP, XLV, VNQ or XLRE, plus similarly liquid covered ETFs only if needed.
- **Product-selection filters:** Alpaca/qfa daily OHLCV coverage; corporate-action/distribution-calendar availability with ex-date and preferably announcement/knowledge timestamp; median dollar-volume sanity; exposure diversity across Treasury duration, credit, aggregate bonds, munis, REITs, and defensive sectors.
- **Selected universe:** To be selected by the research subagent before performance review based on coverage and timestamp-safe distribution data.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Broad enough to distinguish event mechanics from simple carry/duration/defensive allocation; limitations include corporate-action coverage, price-adjustment conventions, dividend cash accounting, and ETF inception/coverage truncation.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV plus timestamp-safe Alpaca corporate-action/distribution data if available. No CSV; no `--data-csv`; no daemon; no orders. If timestamp-safe event data are unavailable, block/reject honestly rather than infer ex-dates from price drops.
- **Signal features:** Separately test pre-ex drift/pressure, ex-date/next-day reversal, and post-payable/reinvestment sleeves only when event timing is known before signal time; event amount/yield if available; raw/split-adjusted price return plus explicit dividend cash treatment where needed.
- **Model form:** Rule-based long-only or market-neutral-lite ETF allocator with cash fallback; sleeves reported separately and combined only if each adds evidence.
- **Parameters / search space:** Event window offsets around ex-date/payable date, maximum holding period 1-10 days, minimum event yield threshold, top-N 1-4, event-family filters, turnover/cooldown constraints.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-weight or event-yield/risk-scaled positions with max sleeve concentration and cash fallback.
- **Risk constraints:** 5/10/20 bps cost sensitivity; avoid holding through events unless dividend cash accounting is explicit; reject if one ETF/family dominates.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** Use real returned history with random windows and event-year stratification; include rates/inflation, benign, stress, and recovery regimes where coverage permits.
- **Metrics:** Median/mean/p25/worst Sharpe, annualized return/volatility, max drawdown, turnover, activation/event count, positive-window rate, sleeve attribution, event concentration by symbol/month/regime.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 5, 10, and 20 bps one-way turnover-cost proxies; 10 bps primary decision threshold.
- **Ablations:** No event calendar / same-universe carry rank; randomized event dates within month; ex-date sleeve only; payable-date sleeve only; high-yield/credit only vs Treasury/aggregate/defensive; raw-return vs dividend-cash-adjusted diagnostics; month-end/turn-of-month baseline.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare against AR-037, AR-049, AR-051, AR-063, AR-060, AR-061, AR-067, AR-114, AR-118, ETF TSMOM, simple carry/duration, month-end/turn-of-month, and naive post-ex-date baseline where returns are available/proxyable.

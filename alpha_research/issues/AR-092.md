## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** BTC/ETH trend, realized volatility, and drawdown state can proxy global risk appetite and lead short-horizon allocation among QQQ/SPY/IWM, duration, gold, and defensive ETFs.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Crypto trades continuously and may react earlier to liquidity/risk sentiment than US cash equities; weekend crypto moves may especially encode Monday risk appetite.
- **Expected failure mode:** Crypto is too reflexive/noisy, relationship is unstable after costs, or signal is a disguised equity beta/momentum clone.
- **Falsifier:** Median random-window Sharpe <= 0 after costs, p25 Sharpe materially negative, unstable across random windows, unavailable external data with timestamp discipline, or high redundancy with existing ETF momentum/defensive alphas.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Crypto-risk-appetite beneficiaries and hedges: BTC/ETH or crypto proxies where supported, high-beta tech, semis, fintech/payments, crypto-linked equities/miners if available, broad equity ETFs, small caps, credit, duration, gold, energy, and defensive/cash-like sleeves.
- **Product-selection filters:** Direct sensitivity to crypto/liquidity regime, liquidity, real-data availability, beta diversity, and avoidance of selecting winners using future returns.
- **Selected universe target:** 30-100 products after filtering; include both risk-on beneficiaries and defensive alternatives.
- **Seed universe examples:** SPY, QQQ, IWM, TLT, IEF, SHY, GLD, HYG, LQD, XLE, XLU, XLV, plus liquid semis/fintech/crypto-exposed equities or ETFs if data-supported.
- **External signal source:** Crypto prices from a real provider available to the repo/API layer (Alpaca crypto, Coinbase, Yahoo/Stooq if licensed/allowed) plus Alpaca/qfa real ETF OHLCV; no CSV-backed market-price research.
- **Signal features:** BTC/ETH 1/3/7-day returns, weekend return, realized vol, drawdown from 20/60-day high, BTC-ETH dispersion, equity ETF price confirmation, volatility targeting.
- **Model form:** Rule-based qfa-compatible `generate_signals(context)` returning symbol weights; external signal ingestion must be deterministic, cached only as compact derived features if allowed, and documented.
- **Parameters / search space:** crypto lookback 3/7/20/60 days, weekend gap flag, vol threshold, risk-on/risk-off sleeve weights, rebalance daily/weekly, max ETF weight 0.35.
- **Position sizing:** Gross exposure <= 1.0, no leverage, max single ETF/stock weight per config, SHY/cash-like fallback allowed when signal confidence is low.
- **Risk constraints:** No daemon, no orders, no raw paid/vendor data committed, no secrets, no lookahead from revised external data. If external data cannot be obtained reproducibly, mark blocked rather than fabricating a proxy.

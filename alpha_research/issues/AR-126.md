## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Annual Russell index reconstitution around the late-June effective date creates temporary benchmark-flow pressure in small-cap/large-cap and size-style ETF spreads that partially reverses after the event.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Passive benchmark tracking, pre-positioning, and closing-auction liquidity around Russell reconstitution can produce temporary price pressure in size/style exposures. A fixed public calendar rule may capture flow pressure without inferring constituents from returns.
- **Expected failure mode:** The effect is just generic turn-of-month/quarter-end/OPEX/J June seasonality, size/value momentum or reversal, ETF TSMOM, one or two historical years, or daily OHLCV is too coarse to observe actual index-flow pressure.

## Implementation Plan
- **Candidate product pool:** Broad ex-ante liquid US size/style ETFs and related broad-market proxies with plausible Russell/reconstitution or size-spread exposure: IWM, IWB, VTWO, VTI, IJR, MDY, RSP, IWF, IWD, IWO, IWN, VTV, VB, VBR, VBK, plus diagnostics only if Alpaca coverage/liquidity/history support them.
- **Product-selection filters:** Real Alpaca/qfa daily OHLCV coverage, sufficient history across June event years, liquidity/dollar-volume proxy, economic exposure to size/style spreads, no performance-based symbol selection.
- **Selected universe:** To be fixed by the research subagent before performance review. Separate broad size ETFs, style ETFs, and any diagnostic sleeves. Single-name sleeves are optional only if fixed before performance review with timestamp-safe membership/coverage rules; otherwise omit.
- **Universe-selection rationale / limitations:** Size/style ETFs are tradable proxies for Russell reconstitution flow pressure but do not identify actual additions/deletions or auction imbalance. ETF histories and product launches may limit usable annual event count.
- **Data source:** qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only. No CSV, no `--data-csv`, no daemon, no orders.
- **Signal features:** Timestamp-safe public calendar rule: last Friday in June / nearby trading days as Russell effective-date proxy. Pre/post event size-spread returns, event-window pressure, and fixed post-event reversal windows. Do not infer index additions/deletions from realized returns.
- **Model form:** Rule-based annual event-calendar allocator on predeclared size/style ETF spreads with fixed entry/exit windows.
- **Parameters / search space:** Primary trade must be predeclared before evaluation: post-effective-date reversal in small-vs-large and/or style/size spread over a fixed 3-10 trading day hold. Alternative windows are diagnostics only.
- **Position sizing:** Equal-risk or equal-weight spreads/sleeves with gross exposure cap and cash when no event window is active.
- **Risk constraints:** 10 bps primary one-way turnover cost; 5 and 20 bps sensitivity; reject if fewer than 12 usable annual events remain, if one/two years dominate, or if performance disappears ex-month-end/ex-OPEX.

## Evaluation Plan
- **Random-period protocol:** Event-year study across all usable Junes with leave-one-year-out, crisis-year/year-dominance diagnostics, matched non-June last-Friday placebo windows, and event-window randomization/holdout diagnostics.
- **Metrics:** Median/mean/p25/worst event Sharpe or event-return distribution where Sharpe is low-power, annualized return/vol where meaningful, max drawdown during event holds, turnover, hit rate, positive-year rate, sleeve-level and combined results.
- **Costs/slippage assumptions:** 10 bps primary; 5 and 20 bps stress.
- **Ablations:** AR-006/AR-021 turn-of-month, AR-106 OPEX/rebalance, AR-125 month-end stock-bond rebalance, ETF TSMOM AR-015/104, AR-123 January/tax-loss style calendar reversal, no-signal June seasonality, non-June last-Friday placebo, simple size/value momentum and reversal, SPY/IWM/IWB standalone baselines.
- **Orthogonality checks vs alpha library:** Compare available compact/proxy return streams against AR-006/021, AR-015/104, AR-106, AR-123, AR-125, AR-016/039/051 defensive rotation, and simple size/value spread proxies where available.
- **Acceptance threshold:** At 10 bps, median/event-return distribution must be positive, p25 not materially negative, positive-year rate above 50%, and primary rule must beat June/no-signal, ToM/quarter-end/OPEX, size momentum/reversal, and ETF TSMOM baselines. Reject if max relevant correlation > 0.60 where comparable streams are available or if fewer than 12 event years are usable.

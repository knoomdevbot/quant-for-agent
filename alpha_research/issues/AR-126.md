## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: After standard monthly equity option expiration, liquid large-cap single names that were unusually range-compressed and sector-residual muted during the pre-OPEX week may show short-horizon directional continuation in the direction of their first post-OPEX sector-residual move as hedging/pinning pressure is released.
- Economic / behavioral intuition: OPEX can concentrate dealer/investor hedging and pinning pressure. If a stock was compressed into expiry, the first post-event residual move may reveal direction once mechanical hedging pressure fades.
- Expected failure mode: The effect is just generic range breakout, liquidity/close-volume reversal, sector beta, earnings contamination, or a noisy calendar artifact that fails matched non-OPEX placebo Fridays and costs.

## Implementation Plan
- Candidate product pool: Fixed ex-ante liquid US large-cap/common-stock pool, approximately 80-150 names if Alpaca/qfa coverage allows; exclude ETFs/funds/REITs from the primary sleeve. Use sector ETF proxies for residualization and diagnostics: XLB, XLC, XLE, XLF, XLI, XLK, XLP, XLRE, XLU, XLV, XLY, plus SPY/QQQ controls.
- Product-selection filters: Real Alpaca daily OHLCV availability, sufficient daily history, high median dollar volume, common-stock primary sleeve where feasible, sector proxy mappability. Do not select symbols based on returns.
- Selected universe: To be finalized by evaluator before performance review using only liquidity, data availability, and economic exposure; target 80-150 liquid large caps if available.
- Universe-selection rationale / limitations: Broad large-cap single-name universe is matched to stock-level option-expiry pinning/release. Static current large-cap lists may have survivorship bias; evaluator must record final symbols and limitations.
- Data source: qfa/Alpaca real daily OHLCV only, plus deterministic timestamp-safe public standard monthly OPEX calendar dates. No CSV, no daemon, no orders.
- Signal features: Pre-OPEX 5-10 trading day range compression vs trailing 60-120 day range; low absolute sector-residual return/range into OPEX; first post-OPEX close-to-close sector-residual move; optional SPY/sector trailing-vol risk brake.
- Model form: Rule-based event-calendar residual continuation.
- Parameters / search space: Compression lookback 5/10d; normalization 60/120d; post-OPEX confirmation 1 day; holding period 3/5d; sector cap/sector-neutral variants; cost 5/10/20 bps sensitivity.
- Position sizing: Equal-risk/equal-weight long-short or beta/sector-capped sleeve among qualifying names; normalize gross exposure; no leverage beyond model weights.
- Risk constraints: Sector caps, max gross exposure normalization, turnover/cost stress, optional no-trade if too few names qualify.
